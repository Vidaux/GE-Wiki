Feso, is the middle currency in SoTnW. What does it mean? simple,Simple, feso is the money that you will use to buy stuff from the Feso shop (which has many of the items that are available in the Cash shop, and even other items only available there).

You buy Feso with Gold, or farming it from some bosses. How is this possible?

With that.

You can exchange a Great StonesStone for 100k Feso; these stones can be purchased in packs in the Cash shop so fesos will move pretty nicely in the Market manager. Just like vis, feso iscan tradebe able.traded. There are other stones that give much less feso (like the sun stone which gives 1k only) and can be obtained by killing bosses or treasure mobs in FA and AA.

The order of things would look like this;

Gold > Feso < Vis

You can buy feso with gold, and you can buy feso with items that can be obtained with vis in the market manager. The exact price of "Feso" in vis can change depending on the economy in each server; the price of Feso in Gold is fixed.

So, where's the Feso Shop?
In any of the cities, you can find her near the cash shop merchant.
Fesos are primarily used to purchase pet food and temporary costumes that give small buffs, such as +500hp when worn.

That's her.
