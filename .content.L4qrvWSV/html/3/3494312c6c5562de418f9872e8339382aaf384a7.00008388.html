So how do you do it?

1. Go to Shattered Crystal's Sword of the New World page and click on the G1 Credits you'd like to purchase in quantities of 400, 800, 1200, 1600 and 2000!

* For your protection, if you are a first time user shatteredcrystal.com will CALL you before completing your purchase. This allows them to verify your identity with a very quick and easy call.

2. Go to www.gamersfirst.com and log into your G1 Account (if you do not have a G1 Account or have not linked your Sword of the New World account go here to find out how).

* If you cannot find your link code on the Sword of the New World website, try re-validating your email address.
* If you can't remember your secret question and answer try contacting customer support here.

3. Click Refill (it's the orange button on the right hand side of the screen), and select ESN from the payment choices.
4. Fill in your ESN number and passcode.
5. Go to the G1 Marketplace and spend your new G1 Credits on Sword or any of our K2 Network games!

* Make sure when spending your G1 credits, you are using it on your linked SWORD ACCOUNT not your G1 ACCOUNT!

So grab some gold and take advantage of our current Cash Shop Sale on Upgrade Accelerators and Enchanting Boosts!!!
