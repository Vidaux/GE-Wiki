________________________
Gerero is one of the first high level raid bosses, he spawns every 5 hours (need confirmation on this) at the last room of the Prison of Joaquin (he can spawn in any area of that map).

 Recomended Classes: All the classes are good for this raid.

Character Instructions:
The tank:
  • Elite Armor level 84 (metal would make the raid easier)
  • High Guard or Defender
  • Bring lots of potions and ignorekeep your scout,scout youalive onlyand wantout toof keepthe way, so that if your fighter alive.gets stunned you can run up and use cure all to prevent him from dying

The Members:
  • DPS is necesary, so any equipment level 92+ or elite 84 that hits fast works.
  • Wizards keep staffs equipped and dont use buffs (they bring lag), Spam Apocalypse and Hole of Darkness to stun the boss.
  • Elementalists use lightning, and spam the raid to electrify it.
  • Scouts DO NOT Revive the tank's characters, keep reviving the fallen "members" and don't stay in defense mode.
  • Scouts DO NOT use Fortify in this raid; remember you want DPS.
  • Musks will only do dps in this raid, so stay a little bit far of the boss and just attack it.

Recomendations:
  • Weapons that stun or give lighting are very usefull
  • A Scout with Intensify ring and Accelerando ring.
  • The Gaze of Fallen Angel (unique staff level 92) is good here, since a hole of darkness lvl 11 has more chance to stun.
  • Apocalypse Ring

Instrucctions:
Once he spawns:
  2. Don't Attack it unless you're the tank.
  3. Once you (the tank) attack it first, you will see a mesage of Gerero saying "Arrggh *player* I will kill you".
  4. Now that you have his attention, take him to the first room of the floor to avoid the other mobs and stay by any of the corners.
  5. After the tank has his aggro (attention) let the members of the party attack it and keep distance.
  6. Defense mode is not a good idea if you have scouts in your family, so turn defense mode off and play your family in individual mode.
  7. MEMBERS do NOT use attacks that could lag the tank (photom splash, meteor strike, etc) ONLY use single target attacks and don't use provoke unless the tank dies and you are the spare tank.
