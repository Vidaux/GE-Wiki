Baron mode is in other words - Player Killing mode.

For you to be able to use Baron mode, you need family level 5, then right click another family with fam lvl 5 or above and select Force Attack(Baron mode). Once you click on force attack, you will be marked with a red skull above your family, and EVERYONE can attack you. If you die in Baron mode, you will lose an item from your inventory (you won't loose equiped items if you stay below baron level 5,3, if you go over that limit there is a chance that they will drop) and sometimes some exp points.

When you kill someone you will get baron points, a baron level and a buff (decreses def, and increses atk), this buff increses with every baron level you gain.

To lower your baron value (points), and get out of baron mode, you need to kill monsters of your level until the baron value goes back to 0.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Baron City - Los Toldos

Los toldos is the home city of the player killers, once you enter baron mode, if you go to the quarters or get killed, you will be sent to "Los Toldos". Here you can get unique items, and some quests.

If you talk to the npc (not the merchant) you can get the following costumes, if you have the necesary points and items.
  • lvl 1 (1 baron points) no named tomb stone - The first one's free.
  • extra tomb stones, cost 5 soul crystals.
  • lvl 2 (2500 baron points)bloody coffin - 1 fanatic costume, 7 soul crystals
  • lvl 3 (5000 baron points) deadtly cross - 1 plate mail costume, 10 soul crystals
  • lvl 4 (10000 baron points) ghost - cross, 13 soul crystals
  • lvl 5 (20000 baron points) wheel of destiny - cross and ghost


Item Buffs:
  1. Tombstone- nothing
  2. Bloody Coffin- DEF -5
  3. Deadly Cross- DEF -10
  4. Ghost- DEF -15, HP +800
  5. Wheel of Destiny- DEF -20, HP +800, AR +1, DR-1

_____________________

Other info about Los Toldos:
  • Being Baron has its ups and downs; one of the things you can enjoy from Los Toldos, are the Baron Elite weapons and Baron UPCs.
  • Land of the Dead can only be accesed through Los Toldos (you need 1 mystery powder each time you want to enter)
