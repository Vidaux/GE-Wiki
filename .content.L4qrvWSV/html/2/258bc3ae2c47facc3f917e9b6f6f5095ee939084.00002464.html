Hellena's Forgotten Magic Stance




UNIQUE SKILL: Summon Prost
Calls upon Prost to assist you in battle. Damage increases with level.
SP Cost: 350
Item Used: 10 Ancient Star Orbs


Comments: She is an upcoming UPC who appears in 3.3 (Note: her actual release as a upcUPC is 3.4)

There are two types of Special Bracelets: Lord of Elemental (LOE), and Skullic. The Lord of Elemental Bracelet, in the case of Hellena, grants access to the Forgotten Magic, as well as the three basic Possession, stances. The Skullic Bracelet is a non-elemental proxy bracelet that can be used in place of the second bracelet for all Evocation and Domination stances
