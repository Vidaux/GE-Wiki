How to:
The Baron Vet weapons (level 92), can be crafted by the npc in los toldos (Gloomy old man). He sells the recipes for 1m each. To get all the materials for these weapons, you will need to complete the Kurt/Eduardo quest. Note that these weapons are for Veterans only.

You can find the FaQ at the bottom of this list, that will explain more about these weapons and how to make them.

About Baron Shields:


Baron shields provide the highest base DEF and Block among all accessible shields in game right now. (The only shield that is better is the expert shield Phantom Guard with 27 DEF, 15 Block, and inherent +2 DR.)

These baron shields have 27 DEF and 13 inherent Block. On top of that, they have 25 resistance to a certain element depending on which one you make.

And the best part is they glow and have special effects! No other shield does that.


Castor & Octagon - Ice
Pollux & Rouge - Fire
Aquila & Noir - Lightning

FAQ:

Q:What's the price of each recipe?
A: It cost 1m per recipe.

Q: Can I sell those recipes?
A: These recipes cannot be traded or discarded.

Q: Where can i find pure otite?
A: After completed the quest given from Gloomy old man, you'll receive 1~2 pure otite as rewards.

Q: Where can I find Spirit Stone?
A: Throw a Fake Unique into anvil and you'll receive a Spirit Stone.

Q: Where can I find Steel Spirit Stone?
A: Throw an Elite 84 Steel Series item into anvil and you'll receive a Steel Spirit Stone.

Q: What is Steel Series?
A: Steel Series is a rare Elite 84 weapons. Pizzaro, Dragon Hunter Bayonet, Shaft Hammer is not a Steel Series weapons.

Q: Where can I find Steel Series?
A: You can hunt Steel Series recipe from HellBreaker.

Q: Where can i find the anvil?
A: The anvil is just beside Gloomy old man in Los Toldos.

Q: Which NPC's should I find to craft it?
A: You have to find Gloomy old man to craft it in Los Toldos.

Q: What is Elite Wave of Steel?
A: Elite Wave of Steel is a Special types of Rod like The Rod of Armageddon and The Grim-stick.
