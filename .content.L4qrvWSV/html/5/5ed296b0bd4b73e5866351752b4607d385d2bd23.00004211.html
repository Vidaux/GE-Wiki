Character Level: 16
Location: Queen's Gate
Job: Explorer
Armor: Leather Armor
Weapon Usage: Single Dagger as sword, and sword as two handed weapon
Status:
Str Agi Con Dex Int Cha
5 8 4 5 3 3
Stances:
Main Gauche - Back Guard, Middle Guard
 Sword - Plow Guard, Roof Guard, Sidewinder

 How to get him:
Level requirement - 1
  1. Talk to Ramiro at Queen's Gate. He will ask you to help defend a girl for 5 minutes. Mobs will come from 3 directions. Survive for 5 minutes to complete the mission.
[Rewards: 3x Lvl 1 (360 Exp) Cards]

Level requirement - 8
  1. Talk to Ramiro. .This time, you will have to defeat him within 4 minutes.
  2. Now return to Ramiro to recieve your card.

Note: When you want to use his Back Guard and Middle Guard stances, you must drag the main gauche to the weapon slot; double clicking wont work.
