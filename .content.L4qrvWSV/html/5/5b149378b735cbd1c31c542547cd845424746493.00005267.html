    Location: Viron
    NPC: Detective Gertrude
    Reward: None

    Name: Master Key (1)
    Location: City of Auch
    NPC: Detective Gertrude
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Go to Reboldeaux.
    2. Purchase 100 Composite Steel and 100 Cast Iron from Deon the Smith.
    3. Return to Gertrude.

    Name: Master Key (2)
    Location: Viron

    Reward: None

    2. Try to enter the Clocktower again.

    NPC: Genia

    Name: The Door that Won’t Open (2)
    Location: Viron
    NPC: Genia
    Reward: None
    Details:
