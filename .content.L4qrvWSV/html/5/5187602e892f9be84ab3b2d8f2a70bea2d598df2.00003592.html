    Port of Coimbra (Tutorial)

    -Store Trade Tutorial
    --Prerequisites:Transference Report
    --Trigger: Speaking with Adjutant for Coimbra Colonization during Transference Report
    --Optional?:Yes
    --Quest NPC(s):Adjutant of Coimbra Colonization; Store Trade Guard
    --Place:Port of Coimbra (Tutorial)
    --Compensation:(10) Food for beginners
    --Completion: Follow the arrows and talk to Store Trade Guard; Read 'Item Purchasing Method' and 'Item Selling Method' if you're new, otherwise choose 'I will try an actual transaction' and purchase (1) Rum;

    -Quarters Mode Tutorial
    --Prerequisites:Transference Report
    --Trigger: Speaking with Adjutant for Coimbra Colonization during Transference Report
    --Optional?:Yes
    --Quest NPC(s):Adjutant of Coimbra Colonization; Quarters Mode Guard
    --Place:Port of Coimbra (Tutorial)
    --Compensation:(10) Mitridarts
    --Completion:Follow the arrows and talk with Quarters Mode guard. Choose 'Character Creation and Character Deletion', 'To Create or Delete a Team', and/or 'Expanding your Quarters' to learn about those things, otherwise choose 'Organize a Team'. You will then be returned to your Quarters to create (if necessary) and team with 2 other family members. Add them to your team and 'Start with new Team'. Return and speak with Quarters Mode Guard to receive your reward. He will ask if you would like to return to Quarters Mode one more time. Choose 'I Won't' to avoid or 'Yes, I would like to return to Quarters Mode.' to do so.

    -A Call from Nunez
    --Prerequisites:Transference Report; Party of 3 family members
    --Trigger: Speaking with Adjutant for Coimbra Colonization during Transference Report
    --Optional?:No
    --Quest NPC(s):Adjutant of Coimbra Colonization; Nunez
    --Place:Port of Coimbra (Tutorial)
    --Compensation:None
    --Completion:Talk with Nunez. Choose 'Go around Coimbra to get trained' to do the previous quest if you haven't already, otherwise choose 'I will go directly to the Reboldoeux Colonization Assistance Center.' to leave Port of Coimbra (Tutorial).
