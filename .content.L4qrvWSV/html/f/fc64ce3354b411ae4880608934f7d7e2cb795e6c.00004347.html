    Reboldeaux Soldier



    -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Character Level    : 1
    Location: Reboldeaux
    Job: Soldier
    Armor: Leather Armor, Metal Armor
    Weapon Usage: Polearm, sword, blunt weapons
    Status:
    Str Agi Con Dex Int Cha
    6 5 6 4 3 3
    Stances:
     Pole Arm - Blandir Cruz, Penetrar Cruz
     Sword + Shield - Back Guard, High Guard
     Blunt Weapon + Shield - The Defender

     How to get Reboldeaux Soldier:
    Level requirement - 1
    1. Talk to the Reboldeaux Soldier standing to the right of Sir Lyndon. You will have to defeat 5 Reboldeaux Soldiers.
    2. Return to the soldier to receive your Reboldeaux Soldier Card.
