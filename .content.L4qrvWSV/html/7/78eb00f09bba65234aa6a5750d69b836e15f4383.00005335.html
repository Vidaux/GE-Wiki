This wiki, like all wikis, is a nonstop work in progress. We need your help to fill in the wiki. So if you have any information that helps the site, add it. If you find anything about the wiki that you think should be changed but don't have the privileges to change (i.e. page movement), let us know. See below for our contact information.

Dec 12: The Game is Dying ZOMG!!!11!1
Not!

The game's dead-line was pretty much the topic these past days. Fear not, the game is not going anywhere; there were some changes inside k2 that had some different effects to the community, and sadly some of the effects brought fear and crazyness. A lot of people left the game, and others were playing just to hear some decent info on swords future.

Well, let me try to explain what's been happening and what's going to happen.
Following the changes inside K2, the info about the game's updates will come slowly and since the forums will be having some changes it will take a pretty decent amount of time to get "core" inside info. For now the game's expansion was already discussed, we saw and have the information about the UPCs that will come and the Expert Stances, plus we are still missing the last part of our latest expansion (The claire, and the rest of calypso's quests). The thing is that since all that was explained early, they don't really have anything new to say; so we end up with a silent discussion. Now, don't go around saying "Oh, but that's not True!", "I want More INFO!!", "Give me a donkey!"; cause they will surely give us new events and the rest of the content soon. =P we just have to sit, and wait.

"K2-Neume
I'm not sure what to say. All the information is already out there. We are having technical issues and don't want to patch to the live servers. We could do that, but then the live servers would have issues. Our choice now is to wait for a fix. Giving ETA's don't really happen anymore until we are 98% sure and at this point we're not there yet.

As for the future, nothing has changed. We are making plans for many activities and events in the new year. It seems to be an annual tradition for players to get "ancy" about the game in the closing months of the year. This same situation occurred last year and we've launched 3 expansions and 8 major updates not to mention bug fixes and more.

So everyone take a breath the sky isn't really falling "

Remember that the forums, always have great info; just use the search function and find the "keywords" =p Think of it like a detective game.

The time is almost here, SoTnW next expansion will come to us on the 15th. The servers will go down early and the maintenance will be a little longer, but is going to be worth it.

You pay them once, and get the service for the days the pack say.

The packs are:

  • 8000 gold
