Abertal Veteran Quests


Investigate Celemantis
NPC: Toubkal Plateau Investigator, Abertal (D8)
Reward: 3x Polish: Veteran and 5x Mystic Spinelles
1. Talk the the Investigator and select [*] Investigate Celemantis.
2. Go to Via Fluvial and kill Celemantis to receive 100 Slough of Celemantis (semi-rare).
3. Go back the the Investigator and deliver the quest.


Investigate Imago
NPC: Toubkal Plateau Investigator, Abertal (D8)
Reward: 3x Polish: Veteran and Key Chain of Mine
Requirements: Complete Investigate Celemantis
1. Talk to the Investigator and select [*] Investigate imago
2. He will ask for following items:
  • 50 Skins of Imago
  • 50 Legs of Imago
  • 50 Rock Imago
3. These can be found in Deprimida Valley by killing Celegrimworms. These are semi-rare, however Rock Imagos are the hardest to get.
4. Go back to Albertal and talk to the Investigator to finish quest


Berry of the Devil
NPC:Toubkal Plateu Investigator, Abertal (D8)
Reward: 3x Polish: Veteran, 5x Mystic Amber and 2x Exp Card (L90 100k)
Requirements: Complete Investigate Imago
1. Talk to the Investigator and select [*] Berry of the devil
2. He will ask you to collect 100 Food of Capybara. These are the normal items dropped by any type of Capybara monster. Also meaning you can buy these from Market Manager. These drop a lot faster from Capybara in Capybara Plantation and the Mine maps than from Abertal.
3. When you have collected 100 or more of these talk to Investigator again to gain reward


A Bear that uses Tools?
NPC: Toubkal Plateau Investigator, Abertal (D8)
Reward: 3x Polish: Veteran and a Rucksack (back costume)
1. Talk to the Investigator and select [*]A bear the uses tools?
2. You must hunt following items:
  • 50 Bear's Napsack
  • 50 Bear's Hoe
3. Bear Workers can be found at Deprimida Valley and Via Fluvial.
4. Go back to the Investigator and deliver quest.

Mission: Exploring the Closed Underground Mine
NPC: Toubkal Mine Investigator, Abertal (G11)
Reward: Admirar's Diary, 3x Polish: Veteran, 10 Fire Orbs and 1 Lightning Orb.
1. Talk to the Investigator at G11 in Abertal.
2. He will ask you to collect Old Journals 1 - 9 from monsters in Abertal, Via Fluvial and Deprimida Valley. Old Journal 10 is a Hell Breaker only drop.
3. Head to Federigo in Auch I-9 with all 10 Journals and hand them in to him. He'll also provide you with some information regarding the three Elemental Gates.

NOTE: These Elemental Orbs are necessary for opening the warp to Fire Gate and Lightning Gate. If you plan on joining a raiding clan, do not ever sell, NPC or give these away as they are limited. You can rarely find these orbs in their corresponding Gate, ie. Fire Orbs may drop from the monsters in Fire Gate, etc.


Capybara Guide for the lost
Thanks to Arconis

Lvl 1 Capybara Plantation
Things of interest:
1)Food Pack-Need 200 food of capybara to use
2)Golden Bat-Mini boss
3)Key of Capybara-Golden bat drop these keys
4)Reward/Treasure Chest-Need 1 key of capybara to open
5)Marker Stone-Need 4 key of capybara to open (All chnls)


Firstly i will give you all the overview.The golden bat is a tough boss to beat.To beat him you have to be tougher than you are now by using the foodpack; this will give you a buff that reduces damage received from the Golden Bat. To get the buff, simply put Food of Capybara into the food pack until there are 200 inside. The family that puts the 200th piece of food inside will receive the buff which lasts around 10 minutes. Once you beat the boss he will drop a random amount of Key of Capybara. As low as 1 key and as high as 6 keys! (BloodAngels got 6 ;p ) The key then can be used to open the reward box for random crap or to clear the marker stone to open the path to enter lvl 2 of the map.

Lvl 2 Mine Passage
Things of interest:
1)Food Pack-Need 200 Food of Capybara to use
2)Golden Bat-Mini boss
3)Key of Capybara-Golden bat drop these keys
4)Locker/Reward/Treasure Chest-Need 1 key of Capybara to open
5)Marker Stone-Need 4 key of Capybara to open (Ch. 1 only)
6)Underground Locker-Mine Diaries are found inside (Quest item)
7)Electric tin mine-Pebbles will be dropped if u click it (Item needed for quest)
8)Gulfuxxi-Rare Mini Boss

*Quest that can be done in this map*
A NPC at Albertal will give you a quest (lvl 95 and above) that requires you to look for mine diaries. You need to find 10 such diaries which are all located at lvl 2 mine passage. Once you gather all mine diaries, talk to him again and he will give you another quest, which is to look for 20 pieces of tourmaline (pebbles). This quest is repeatable. The first 20 pebbles you deliver,will be rewarded with 300K vis,1 lvl 92 enchantment chip, and 3 lvl 90 exp cards. Subsequent deliveries of 20 pebbles will be rewarded with 5 diamond coupons and 1 lvl 92 enchantment chip.

Repeat the same process as lvl 1 to proceed to lvl 3. You will need another 4 keys of Capybara to go to the third level, Toukbal Mine.


Lvl 3 Toukbal Mine
Things of interest:
1)Food Pack-Need 200 food of Capybara to use
2)Golden Bat-Mini boss
3)Key of Capybara-Golden bat drop these keys
4)Mine excavator switch - Need to use 2 keys of Capybara to activate 1 switch, there's 2 switches (2 minutes limit to activate second switch) -To summon hellbreaker-raidboss
5)Main power - Destroy this structure to temporarily deactivate the irritating Capybara turrets.
6)Gulfuxxi-Rare Mini Boss

Proceed to the big platform. Go up the stairs until you reach the two small paths leading to the center. There will be Capybara turrets there on each side. If there are no blades, proceed inside the platform. To summon Hellbreaker, you need to use 4 key of capybara, 2 for each excavator switch. Once you have used the keys, 3 things will happen:
  1. Hellbreaker will be summoned near the main power area
  2. A timer will begin counting down,starting from 720 minutes (12 hours)
  3. Rotating blades will appear at the path to stop people from entering or leaving the platform, thus making Hellbreaker impossible to KS.

*Notes*
If the rotating blades are at the path, do not run through them! The rotating blades are there to stop other players from interfering with the raid. The rotating blades are only present if Hellbreaker has been summoned, so your only choices are to leave or wait for the timer to count down.

Hellbreaker is a flying enemy.
Hellbreaker has 10 million HP
Collect 12 keys of Capybara at level 1 before going to levels 2 and 3; this way you don't have to bother fighting the golden bats at each level.
Once you've used the keys, they will be consumed.
You cannot save a warp spot inside at levels 2 and 3. If you get disconnected, move to the barracks, or log off, you cannot come back via 'Start With Previous Team'.
Once you've used the keys on the marker stone, the path will remain open temporarily.
Collect all of the mine diaries and pebbles at level 2 so you won't waste the keys being used to open the 2nd level.
You can change channels at levels 1 and 2 only.
