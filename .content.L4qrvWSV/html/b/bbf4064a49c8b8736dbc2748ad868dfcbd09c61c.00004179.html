    Raids Faqs:

    • What is a Raid?
    1. Raids are a kind of "quest" that you can do with a group of people to get special items. Usually the objective of a raid is to kill the specific boss; so the drop can be obtained. The Raids are a part of any serious clan because without the raids you can't get the end game gear, and therefor can't complete the game.

      • We killed the raid boss, and the boss dropped 4 items and about 20 people participated in the raid. Who should get items?
      1. The best way to distribute the items among the players is by using Fireworks to get random numbers; the player with the highest number will get the bided item. Note that this way is sometimes unfair, since a player that didn't do much can get a drop. So the best way is doing it with the next method.
      2. For Clans: You can use a raid point system, and let the leader give items to the players that need them. See Raid point system.
      3. The player that's tanking will be priority when selecting the items.
      4. If the raid is being completed by 2 or more clans, use the Fireworks first; then distribute the items to the members using the Raid point system.

        • Can I solo a raid boss?
          1. Yes, 2 of the high level bosses can be soloed if you have the right equipment. Also the low level dungeon bosses count as raid bosses if you are near their level, so you can solo those with enought level.
        • The boss didn't spawn, what happened?
          1. The spawn time of each raid boss is different; some of them only spawn 2 times a day. Check their guides for more info.
        • I have more questions, were do I ask them?
          1. Just start a new Thread with the question, and we will answer it at soon as possible.
