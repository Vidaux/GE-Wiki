    Character Level: 20
    Location: Reboldeaux, Queen's Gate
    Job: Jack's Niece
    Weapon Usage: Construction Hammers
    Status:
    Str Agi HP Dex Int Cha
    4 6 5 6 5 4

    Stances:
    1. Trapper
    2. Construction
    3. Build Structure
    Comments: This quest's pretty expensive if you don't have the return sheet scrolls.
    Required Level -     16

    Quest -
    NPC + Location: Angie, Reboldeaux, Queen's Gate
    Reward: Angie Card
    Difficulty: Expensive
    1. Talk to Angie at Queen's Gate (F9).
    2. Travel to King's Garden to hunt for 30 Skeleton Bones dropped by Skullic Footmen.
    3. Talk to Angie.
    4. You will be teleported to an instance.
    5. Defeat some enemies until Angie finishes building her cannon turrets.
    6. Talk to Angie.
    7. You will be teleported to an instance.
    8. Break the stones.
    9. Talk to Angie.
    10. Hand over 175 return sheets for each town.
    11. Travel to King's Garden.
    12. Hunt Lazim-Lam’s for 50 Lazim-Lam’s red wings.
    13. Talk to Angie.
    14. Talk to Gertrude in Auch.
    15. You will be teleported to an instance. Watch the scenes.
    16. Talk to Angie to receive her card.
    -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Quest Tips
    Total cost for the return scrolls (3 50-scroll bundles per town + 5 5-scroll bundles per town): 1875 gold.
    Source: http://swordofthenewworld.gamersfirst.com/merchant_items.php?cat=4&pg=2
