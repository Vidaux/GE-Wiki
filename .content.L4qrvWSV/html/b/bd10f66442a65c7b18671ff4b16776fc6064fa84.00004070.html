LVL 60

Quest: Iler’s Rococo
NPC + Location: Najib Sharif, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: EXP Card x3, Polish: Explorer x3
Difficulty:easy
1. Talk to Najib Sharif
2. Travel to Dr. Torshe Mansion, Reception Hall
3. Hunt for 30x Antique Silver Candle dropped by Haunted Candle and 30x Rococo Furniture dropped by Haunted Desk
4. Talk to Sharif to complete the quest

Quest: Vera, The Furniture Connoisseur
NPC + Location: Najib Sharif, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: EXP Card x3, Polish: Explorer x3
Difficulty:easy
1. Finish Iler's Rococo.
2. Talk to Najib Sharif
3. Talk to Vera
4. You will be teleported to an instance. Defeat the furniture and defeat the ghost baron that appears afterwards
5. Talk to Vera again
6. Talk to Sharif to complete the quest

Quest: Fix Furniture
NPC + Location: Najib Sharif, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: Najib Sharif’s Card, EXP Card, Polish:Explorer x3 and level 60 Enhancement Chip
Difficulty:easy
1. Finish Vera, The Furniture Connoisseur.
2. Travel to El Tejado Verde
3. Kill Daemonische to get 20x Daemonische Oil from
4. Kill Tree to get 20x Solid Timber
5. Talk to Sharif to complete the quest

Quest: Learning by Close Application! Claude Baudez
NPC + Location: Claude Baudez, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: Durandal, Trooper's Saber, EXP Card, Slayer, Brandistock, Polish: Explorer x3 & Claude Baudez's Card
Difficulty:easy
1. Talk to Claude
2. Travel to Porto Bello - A Deserted Quay to kill Bomber Tortoise to obtain 45x Turtle Shells (rare drops)
3. Travel to El Lago de Tres Hermanas to kill Corlien Dynamiter and Dandelion Gorilla to obtain 45x A Bomber’s Dagger and 45x Tungsten (rare drops)
4. Talk to Claude to receive some items
5. Travel to Dr Torche's Mansion – Savage Garden to kill
Haunted Candle to receive 60x Live Candlestick Fragment with Evil Spirit
Wild Dogs to receive 60x A Wild Dog’s Canine Tooth
Gehcos to receive 60x A Bulk of Tin
6. Talk to Claude to receive some items and complete the quest

Quest: Investigation of Torsche House
NPC + Location: Jean Pierre Gascon, City of Auch
Reward: 30000 Vis + XP Card
Difficulty:easy
1. Talk to Jean Pierre Gascon
2. Travel to Dr Torche's Mansion. To kill Haunted Desks to receive 40 Torche's Diary
3. Talk to Jean Pierre Gascon to complete the quest

Quest: Investigation of Joaquin Concentration Camp
NPC + Location: Jean-Pierre Gascon, City of Auch
Reward: EXP CardCard, 50,000 Vis, and 50,000Polish: VisPioneer
Difficulty:
1. Finish the above Quest
2. Talk to Jean
3. Travel to Prison de Joaquin
4. Search the Gulag Furnaces to receive 10 Jaquin Diaries
Prison de Joaquin, The Mohrgus - I9, D6 & B8
Prison de Jaoquin, Altar of Despair - I11
Prison de Joaquin, Torture Chamber - J5 & B9
Prison de Joaquin, Schivarliere - A6 & B7
Prison de Joaquin, Gehenna Bridge - F3 & F10
5. Talk to Jean to complete the quest

Quest: Investigating of Sir Lyndon
NPC + Location: Jean-Pierre Gascon, City of Auch
Reward: Jean-Pierre Gascon's Card and Polish: Pioneer
Difficulty:
1. Complete the above quest and talk to Jean
2. Travel to Cite de Reboldoeux
3. Talk to Brunie Etienne
4. Give her 5000 Vis
5. Talk to Eusebio (not sure if you have to Talk to Jean first??)
6. Travel to Joaquin de Prison, The Mohrgus and farm for 20x Bulk of Gold
7. Talk to Eusebio
8. Talk to Domingo
9. Talk to Jean to complete the quest
