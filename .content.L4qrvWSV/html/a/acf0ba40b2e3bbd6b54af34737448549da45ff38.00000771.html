    The most famous tailor of Granado Espada. He makes wonderful clothes and is admired by many people around the continent. He's also an excellent swashbuckler, using rapiers and sometimes parrying daggers with great skills. Andre is a most interesting character because he can wear any armor except Metal, has good offensive and tanking stats, and uses the regular version of his Expert stance, making him arguably the best Rapiere user.

    How to get:
    Name: Shattered Dreams and Fantazzies
    Location: Reboldeaux
    NPC: Jin Hee, The Reboldeaux Pioneering Officer.
    Reward: EXP Card (3), Pose Guide Vol. 2, Polish Initiate (3)
    Details:
    1. Speak to Andre (D-8)
    2. Travel to Reboldeaux Stone Pit.
    3. Kill 5 Mandradora.
    4. Return to Andre for reward.

    Name: The Secret Behind the Holy Water
    Location: Reboldeaux
    NPC: Andre
    Reward: EXP card (3)
    Details:
    1. Must have Source of the Taint completed
    2. Talk to Panfilo about the holy water.
    3. Return to Andre for reward.

    Name: Andre Janzur's Gone Crazy
    Location: Reboldeaux
    NPC: Andre
    Reward: EXP card (3), Polish Initiate (3)
    Details:
    1. Accept quest to be transported to an instance.
    2. Duel and defeat Andre.
    3. Return to Andre for reward.

    Name: Sympathy for Dilos Latemn
    Location: Reboldeaux
    NPC: Andre
    Reward: EXP card (3), Polish Initiate (3)
    Details:
    1. Travel to Al Quelt Moreza Arcade.
    2. Look for alter at G4 to be transported to an instance.
    3. Take down the 3 portals, noted by red boxes on map.
    4. Return to Andre for reward.

    Name: Andre In Despair
    Location: Reboldeaux
    NPC: Andre
    Reward: Irolina Hat, Polish Initiate (3)
    Details:
    1. Go to Panfilo
    2. Complete quest Pure White Food. (see Panfilo)
    3. Return to Andre for reward.

    Level Requirement - 72
    Name: Birds of a Feather Flock Together
    Location: Reboldeaux
    NPC: Andre
    Reward: Andre Janzur Character Card, Polish Explorer (3)
    Details:
    1. Travel to Bonavista River.
    2. Kill Cockatrice for requested items. (rare drop)
    3. Return to Andre for reward.

    1. For the last part of the quest, is just better to AFK in a group for some time, when you get back to your pc, you will have the items.
