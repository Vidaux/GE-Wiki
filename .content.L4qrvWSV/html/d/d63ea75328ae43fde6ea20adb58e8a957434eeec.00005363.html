Wizard Guide

Wizard is an Intermediate class.

For a more detailed list of what the abreviations stand for, click here.

PvEG - Medium
PvEP - Low
PvEB - High
PvER - High
PvEF - Medium
PvPS - Medium
PvPL - Medium

(Atk)Psychokinesis > Rod or Staff : Basic Wizard stance.
Strike Back: Nice spammable single target skill.
Web: AoE disabling skill.
Earthquake: Fast casting AoE with medium damage and knockdown effect.
Apocalypse: Huge area AoE with low damage. Disables enemy skills.
This stance is good for any type of situations.
PvEG PvEP PvEB PvER PvEF PvPS PvPL

(Buff)ESP > Rod lvl20: Provides decent defensive buffs.
Spiritual Shield: Provides high Mental RES.
Anti-Magic Shield: Gives high elemental resistance.
Protection Field: Physical defense buff: gives +80 def for 45 seconds on lvl 10.
Dispel Magic: Removes all duffs and debuffs in an area and drops all SP (even your family's) to 0.
PvEG PvEP PvEB PvER PvEF PvPS PvPL

(Debuff)Illusionist > Staff lvl36: Casts debuffs on enemies. Used to slow down atk speed, put them to sleep, reduce their elemental and mental RES up to 75% (Mastered) and can make your team invisible for a short period of time and cannot be seen unless "detected" by a scout (note that most of the mobs can see you even if you are invisible.). Useful debuffs for mobs and PvP situations, can be used on bosses too but not as effective.
PvEG PvEB PvER PvEF PvPS PvPL

(Atk)Levitation > Rod or Staff lvl60: The ultimate stance for a wizard. Allows you to fly. Greatly increases movement speed and attack range. In this stance you also have splash damage.
Reverse: Holds 6 enemies into the air for 12 seconds.
Photon Splash: Nice fireball shooting AoE, 10 targets.
Hole of Darkness: Opens a black hole. 75% chance of stun.
Flying costs constant SP. If your SP drops to 0 you will go back into Psychokinesis stance.
PvEG PvEP PvEB PvER PvEF PvPS PvPL

(Buff)Incantation > Rod lvl100+: The best buffing stance. Contains 2 skills which increase spell evasion. Other skills:
Invulnerability: Blocks all attacks for 7 seconds on lvl 10, 20 second cooldown.
Howling of Heaven: Causes a group of enemies to panic (similar to fear but drops defense to 0).
PvEG PvEP PvEB PvER PvEF PvPS PvPL

(Debuff)Anathema > Staff lvl100+: The best cursing stance.
Confusion: Confuses enemies (100% chance of confusion).
Enervation: Lowers attack and movement speed. Curse: Makes enemies suffer double damage from the attacks.
Stoned Disease: Turns enemies into stone for 40 seconds on lvl 10, but increases targets' defense.
PvEG PvEP PvEB PvER PvEF PvPS PvPL
