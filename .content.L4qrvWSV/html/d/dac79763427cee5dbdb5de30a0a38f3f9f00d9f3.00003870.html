    Note: All quests linked to this page will be linked to the appropriate map pages. Map pages have been updated to show trigger spots. This should make completing some of the quests a little easier.

    Quest by Level

     Pioneering: These are quests that pertain to the main storyline. These quest must be completed in order shown in red. These quests can serve as prerequisites for other quests and UPCs.

    1. Pioneering Level 1-100:
      • Reboldeaux - Start Here
      • Port of Coimbra - Second
      • City of Auch - Third
      • Ustiur Base Camp - Fourth + meet level requirement (78)
    2. Pioneering New Opuluto: Quests related to the Bahamar Swamp. - Fifth
    3. Pioneering Snowfield: Quests related to the Katovic Snowfield area. - Sixth (can do alongside New Opoluto)
    4. Pioneering Caebolan: Quests related to Caebolan and Titan's Remorse. - Cannot do until New Opoluto complete
    5. Pioneering Errac: Quest related to El Dorado area. - Cannot do until New Opoluto complete
    6. Chateau de Bourgogne - Required to gain Hellena and progress into future questlines
    7. Holy Water Chamber - Required to gain Prelude and progress into future questlines
    8. 5 Elemental Otite - Required to gain Raven, Lionel, and Viron quests
    9. Pioneering Viron - Quests related to the Port of Viron.
    10. Viron Clocktower - Required to gain Lionel
    11. Lucifer Castle
    12. Kielce Questline
    13. Return of the Heroes
    14. Bahamar Expedition
      1.-Episode 1
      2.-Episode 2
    15. Armonia Holy City
