The Sword of the new world Granado espada pets, are a must have for any explorer. They give support to make afk and active level grinding easy, some of them auto grab, and others heal better than your scout (since they can't be killed). The Story behind these furry little creatures is still unknown, but who really cares? they are cute enough to eat (no.. really.. their eggs taste great) Read bellow to find out how to get them.

How to get:
Quest: Meeting the Pet Keeper
1. Head to Gigante Beach F6 and talk to the Pet Keeper. He will tell you that he's feeling a little sick and that he would love some food from Panfilo. He understands that Panfilo is to far away, so he doesn't ask you for anything at this point.



2. Talk to him a second time and he will tell you about a home made fried egg. After the dialogue, he will send you to find him "Eggs with Moss" in a secret cave in Fire Isle.

3. So, now you must head off to the first area of Fire Isle and find the Cave. The Cave is strangely hidden in a rock at F10. Click on it to enter the Cave.



4. Once inside the cave, you'll notice some Poppets running around; your mission is to get an egg with moss, which is the proud possession of the Pet Guardian (an oversized pet) so to get him over here. Kill the Poppets that you see running around. Once you kill them, the guardian will appear along with a healer pet and 3 poppets. You'll also see the Guardian Egg. You need to click this after the Pet Guardian disappears. You can either kill all the Pet Guardian's adds to make it disappear or the Guardian itself. The former is much easier. After clicking the egg wait to be warped from the Cave.
NOTE: You have a very limited time to click the egg on time (2-3 seconds after the guardian says "~~")

NOTE: The level of the Guardian is dependent on your character levels. If your highest character is L101 then the Pet Guardian will be L101 as well.



5. Take the egg back to the Pet Keeper to continue your quest line.


Quest: Obtain Egg
1. After you give the Pet Keeper the Egg with Moss talk to him again and help him defend against the pets. They are attacking because they want the egg. Go figure.
2. You'll be warped to an instance. Run over the bridge to the beach and help the Pet Keeper defend the Pet Guardian. His level won't be scaled this time.
3. Talk to the Pet Keeper again to start the next quest.


Quest: Hatch the Egg in the Hatchery
1. The Pet Keeper will ask you for some more help.
2. He'll send you to the glass house at Gigante Beach. The egg will be there in the corner and the room will be filled with small monsters. It will take 3 days for the egg to hatch. This is 3 real life days. So come back to the Hatchery every day to click on the egg. I'm not sure what the point of the rabbits are other than cute things to kill to vent your anger at having to wait 3 days for the egg to hatch.
The next phase will take you to the same room, but this time just click the egg (no need to over kill the little things...)
The third and final phase will be something similar, but this time a reb soldiers will try to kill you; so defeat them and click the egg, for your surprice the egg will hatch.

Go back to the Pet lover, and talk to him; Congrats, that's your pet =P Name him and enjoy it.

Note: If you get more egg with moss, you can take them to the pet keeper and do the last part of the quests and get more pets. If you find a "Vitamin" you can give it to the egg on the 2nd day and turn him into a Moppet (healer type).

Recomendations: and Other info:
  • You can complete the quest easy after lvl 60+ since it seems that the mobs change levels depending on your family team.
  • If you have money, you can just buy the egg from the market manager instead of doing the first quest.
  • The pets have a 5 second action time, that means that they will do their thing once every 5 secs.
  • Pet food last for 1 hour, but you can over-feed your pet if you need to keep them alive over night.


About Pet Food:
You can see the pet interface in the Menu of the game; you might notice that you need pet food for them to work. You can get pet food from the Feso Shop in bundles. So just buy some gold or just sell some items in the market manager for feso and buy some.
