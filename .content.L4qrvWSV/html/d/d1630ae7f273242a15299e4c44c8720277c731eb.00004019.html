Level 44

Quest: Supply T.N.T
NPC + Location: Jose Cortasar, Port of Coimbra
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:
1. Talk to Jose Cortasar
2. Collect 100 High Quality Tortoise Powder by killing Armed Tortoise at either Porto Bello - The Hold or Porto Bello - Deserted Quay.
3. Return to Jose Cortasar for your rewards
Note: This quest took me a long time. Armed Tortoise are not only rare in both areas, they only drop the item about half the time. I found the quickest way to do this quest is to go to the memorial at Deserted Quay and run around that large area until you find an Armed Tortoise. Kill it and move on. Because of the high HP of the turtles, killing everything in your path may significantly extend the length of this quest unless you are high enough level to OHKO everything in the area.

Quest: Taste of the Homeland
NPC + Location: M'boma, Port of Coimbra
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:
1. Talk to M’boma
2. Talk to Panfilo at Cite de Reboldoeux
3. Kill 30 Crate Octopus at Porto Bello (Rare drop)
4. Talk to Panfilo
5. Talk to M'boma to complete the quest

Quest: An Absinian Warrior
NPC + Location: M'boma, Port of Coimbra
Reward: M'boma's Card
Difficulty:
1. Talk to M’Boma
2. Instanced to do battle with him
3. After defeating him, Talk to him again to get his card
Tips: First unleash you most powerfull skills on Claude, this should kill him at once. After this aim for M’Boma. Watch your HP, his attacks do over 1000 damage on my lvl 44 characters. Alternatively, use a musk and spam Birdfall. As soon as M'Boma dies, the quest ends. You don't have to kill Claude.
