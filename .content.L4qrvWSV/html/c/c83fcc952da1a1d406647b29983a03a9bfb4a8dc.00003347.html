    2. A City Under Threat - Required to gain Andre, Claude, Idge character cards. Also to access future raid area.
    3. The Source of Taint     - Required to gain Andre, Claude, Idge character cards. Also to access future raid area.
    4. Menendez Battle - Optional

    Port of Coimbra

    3. The Secret Dock - Optional
    4. The Secret Export of Iron Ore - Optional
    5. Stone Worship - Optional
    6. The Nimraldi Bridge Defense Operation - Optional
    7. The Tetra Ruins - Optional

    Ustier Base Camp
    - Required Level 78 -
    1. Toribio's Proclamation of Love - Required to progress boat trip to Ustiur Base Camp
    2. Rum is a Sailor's Best Friend     - Required to progress boat trip to Ustiur Base Camp
    3. The Queen of Pioneers, Grenmah - Required to progress boat trip to Ustiur Base Camp
    4. Pepito is Out of His Mind - Optional
    5. The Fisherman Arrentos' Gift - Optional, but required to gain access to fish shop
    6. Dinosaur Killer - Optional
    7. Bone Hunter - Optional
    8. The Way to a Farm - Required to gain Romina
    9. Cultivation and Sowing - Required to gain Romina
    10. Special Fertilizer - Required to gain Romina
    11. Fertilizing - Required to gain Romina
    12. The Delivery of a Lunchbox - Required to gain Romina
    13. Season of Harvest     - Optional
    14. Misfortune of Ustiur Farm - Optional
    15. Supply of Corns - Optional
    16. Golden Firearms - Optional, but required to gain access to Arrentos' fish shop quests.
    17. Better Fishing Rods - Optional, but required to gain access to fish shop
    18. Fishing for Bait - Optional, but required to gain access to fish shop
