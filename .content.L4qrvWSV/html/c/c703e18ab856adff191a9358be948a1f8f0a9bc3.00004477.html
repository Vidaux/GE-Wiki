Anonymous Hero Seal 0 Jul 24 2009, 2:57 AM EDT by Anonymous
 
Thread started: Jul 24 2009, 2:57 AM EDT  Watch
Seal is impossible to get without a squad. Even if you made it into poison yard for Chimera, the bosses is not soloable as other players / mobs will be around to mess things up.

I went with a squad of around 7 families all the time, 5 would do if the team is real imba. Chimera will drop only 1~2 Hero Seals.

Poison Yard - 5 ~ 7 veteran families with decent / imba equips, good pvp skills. Definition of imba : elite 84 armors / weapons + 3 dr/ar
Bosses:
1) Geroro - Weak, can even solo but does not drop seals. Drops e84 equips/ 84 and above chips/ e84 recipes (1 ~ 2 spawns per raid)
2) Chimera - Major aoe damage and debuffs like Burn( Can pot, no atk), Frozen( No pot, no atk). Musks with kneeling stances are highly recommended to hit from a safe distance. Fighters without (imba) armor will need at least 500 Healing Liquors. Do not use scouts to heal, spam recover to prevent fighters from freezing. Elementalists won't be of much help, same for wizards. Spawns at center, one only, kill before raid time is up.

Secret Temple - Don't even bother without 8+ family members. Too lazy for guide details.
Battle Colleseum - This may be the easiest way to get it.

Hero Seals ARE NOT tradable.
