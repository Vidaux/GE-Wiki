    • Raising the Attributes Hi =P

      You do raise the attributes, but only after you reach level 100.

      Once you reach lvl 100 and promote your character to Veteran status, you recieve 1 extra point to put in any of the character's attributes.

      You recieve 1 stat point per promotion, and there are 5 total promotions in the game (although you can only reach Expert status for now)

      Veteran - Level 100 to 110
      Expert - Level 110 to 120
      Master - Level 120 to 130
      High Master - Level 130 to 140
      Grand Master - Level 140 to 150

      so in total, once you reach the max level, you will end up with 5 extra attribute points.

      you can check the Character promotion page in the guide section for more info =P
