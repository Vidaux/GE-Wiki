    Found on the final floor of the Secret Tower, also known as Sacred Temple, and is a mission lobby timed raid that may be done at any time.
    Along with the General Guard you may find his two followers that provide him with buffs and hurt you with debuffs. These two are     Betelguese (Red) and (Blue)

    Strategy:
    The strategy for General Guard is pretty much straight forward. If and egg is laid attempt killing is asap. Usually the two Followers are killed before General Guard to make the rest of the fight easier. Of course, Blue is a MUST kill due to its 10% healing skill. Please do note however; that the Followers and Inferior have no drops and it may be possible to just kill the General Guard but this is not confirmed.

    Do note that all of them have AOE spells which may devastate your team if your not careful
