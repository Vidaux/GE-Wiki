3. Quests

Level 1

Quest A Strange Tea Box
  1. After the tutorial, you'll be sent back to the Barracks. Choose your team and enter the game. You'll begin at the Office of Pioneering Support.
  2. Once you reach the Office of Pioneering Support, speak to Sir Llyndon to receive your first quest. (You can press Alt + A to see what quests you currently have in-hand)
  3. He'll send you to Najib Sharif. Head out to Cite de Reboldoeux to look for him.
  4. At Cite de Reboldoeux, head to Najib Sharif. You can look for him on the zone map. (Alt + Z to open zone map)
  5. Speak to him and accept the next part of your quest, which is to look for Vera and get an antique tea box appraised. Use the zone map to find Vera and head to her.
  6. Speak to Vera to get an appraisal of the Tea Box.
  7. Go back to Najib Shariff to complete the quest.
