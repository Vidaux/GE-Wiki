LVL 57

Quest: Intruder Alert!
NPC + Location: - Dr. Torsche Mansion, Reception Hall
Reward: Access to the Maid's Room
Difficulty:Medium,Difficulty: Medium, irritating
1. Defeat Victor to gain access to the maid’s room. The entrance to the room to do battle with him is at Dr. Torsche Mansion, Reception Hall E3
Note: You only have to kill Victor to win. So, if the other monsters aren't aggro...don't make them.

Quest: Help Kamen clean up the place
NPC + Location: Cayenne, The Assistant, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Maid's Room
Reward: EXP Card, Weapon Manufacturing
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. This quest is only available after you have completed Intruder Alert!
2. Talk to Cayenne, The Assistant twice
3. He will request you to help him polish 5 armors
4. They are located at B7, D8, H10, J7, H6 (See map: Reception Hall)
5. Return to him after you have polished all 5 armors to earn your EXP Card
7. He will now be able to help you manufacture a weaponweapons

Quest: Otite Key
NPC + Location: Roseanne the Maid, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Maid's Room
Reward: EXP Card & Access to the Grand Library (2nd Floor)
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. This quest is only available after you have completed Intruder Alert!
2. Talk to Roseanne the Maid
3. Choose “How do I get to the 2nd floor”
4. She will tell you she can manufacture the key for you but she need 200 blue otite pieces. They can be obtain by killing Lloyd, Walter or Sasha (Rare Drops)
After collecting the 200 blue otite pieces. Hand them over to Roseanne the Maid and she will pass you the key and EXP Cards
5. After gathering 200 Blue Otite Piece, go back to Maid Rosanne, you can now access Mansion's Grand Library. Accomplishing this quest allows you to let Rosanne make nifty equips for you.
Torsche Dress (Level 64 Robe) for Female Elementalist - 100 Patch of Dress (dropped by Sasha) and 30 Otite Piece
Maid Uniform for Female Wizard - 100 Patch of Maid Uniform (dropped by Helena) and some Otite Piece
Maid Headband - 25 Patch of Maid Uniform (dropped by Helena) and some Otite Piece
Magical Broom, a Rod for Female Wizard - 1 Broom (Pole arm dropped by Helena) and some Otite Piece.

Quest: Humans Need Food Robots Need Otite
NPC + Location: Kamen, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Grand Library
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. Enter the Grand Library at J8
2. Enter the Butler’s Room at G6 of Grand Library
3. Talk to Kamen
4. Return to Roseanne the Maid
5. This open up the quest “A Power Storage Device for Kamen”
6. After getting the storage power device, return to Kamen to get EXP card

Quest: A Power Storage Device for Kamen
NPC + Location: Rosanne, the maid, Dr. Torsche Mansion, maids room
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. Talk to Roseanne the Maid
2. She say she can fix up a power storage device for Kamen but she requires some material
3. She needs
10x Power Device Cogwheels from Haunted Book
30x Power Device Springs from Helena
20x Power Devices Tubes from Haunted Desk
4. Return to Roseanna the Maid after you collected all the items
5. Hand the items to her to get the power storage device and EXP cards
6. Continue with “Humans Need Food Robots Need Otite!” Quest

Quest: A Mysterious Crimson Ribbon
NPC + Location: Kamen, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Grand Library
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. Go to Dr. Torsche Mansion, Reception Hall Between J10 & I10
2. Click on the painting on the wall to receive the ribbon
3. Talk to Kamen
4. Select “I would actually wanted to talk to you about something else”
5. Ask him how to enter the Laboratory
6. He will then see the ribbon
7. This open up the quest “The Alchemist Torsche”
8. You will receive the key to the laboratory

Quest: The Alchemist Torsche
NPC + Location: Kamen, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Grand Library
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. Go to the laboratory
2. Defeat the waves monsters thrown at you by Dr. Torsche to gain access to talk to Dr. Torsche
Note: The barrels on the side of the room contatin food should you need it.
Note 2: After gaining access to the lab, you might want to make a warp point as you will be going and leaving from their a lot for the Cath quests.

Quest: Catherine’s Spine
NPC + Location: Dr. Torsche, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Laboratory
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:easyDifficulty :easy
1. Talk to Dr. Torsche
2. Select “Does this ribbon look familiar to you?”
3. After a bit of talking, he will ask you to go to Kamen to get Catherine’s Spine
4. After talking to him, you will get EXP card
5. Go to Kamen to get Catherine’s Spine
6. Ask him “Who’s Catherine?”
7. You will get Catherine’s Spine and EXP card after he finish talking
8. Return to Dr. Torsche to get EXP Card and open up the quest “Open Your Eyes! Catherine”

Quest: Open Your Eyes! Catherine
NPC + Location: Dr. Torsche, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Laboratory
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. Talk to Dr. Torsche
2. Boom, Catherine will explode…..
3. Talk to Dr. Torsche again to get EXP Card and open up the quest “It is Best to Prepare for a Rainy Day”

Quest: It is Best to Prepare for a Rainy Day
NPC + Location: Kamen, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Grand Library
Reward: EXP Card
Difficulty:easyDifficulty: easy
1. Go to Kamen
2. Choose “He seems to be hurt”
3. Kamen will then give you Catherine’s Head and EXP Card
4. Return to Dr. Torsche
5. Pick which evenever option applies (I picked the first “Kamen wanted you to have it” )
6. This open up the quest “A Plan to Revive Catherine”
7. You will get EXP Card after talking to him

Quest: A Plan to Revive Catherine
NPC + Location: Dr. Torsche, Dr. Torsche Mansion, Laboratory
Reward: EXP Card x5, Polish: Explorer x9, Catherine card
Difficulty:LongDifficulty: Long or expensive
1. Dr. Torsche needs you to bring him some Catherine’s part
2. He needs Right Arm, Left Arm, Right Hand, Left Hand, Right Leg, Left Leg, Right Foot, Left Foot, Pelvis & Heart
(Heart,right Heart,right hand,right arm -Walter
Left hand,Left leg,Pelvis -Loyd
Left foot,left arm,left leg -Sasha
Body,Right foot,Right leg –Helena)–Helena
3. Place a normal heart at Catherine for Catherine Str, Int or Dex. Place a Refined Quartz as Catherine's heart for Catherine Marionette Control
4. Completed the quest
