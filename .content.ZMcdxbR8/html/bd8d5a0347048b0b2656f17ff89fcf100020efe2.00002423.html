Comments: He's pretty cool, and hits pretty hard. The Best Detective in the new world.

 How to get:
Level requirement - 60
  1. Talk to Gurtrude. He will ask you to collect 40 Torche's Diary from Flying Books (lvl 59) at Dr Torche's Mansion.
  2. [Rewards: 30,000 vis & 1x Lvl 60 (43,600 Exp) Card]

Level requirement - 68
  1. Talk to Grutrude again. This time, he wants you to collect 10 Jaquin Diaries. They can be gotten by talking to the statues/stone pillars found on all 5 levels of the Prison. Here are the locations:
  2. 1st Level - B8, I9 & (D5)
  3. 2nd Level - J11 & (C2)
  4. 3rd Level - B9 & J5
  5. 4th Level - A6 & B7
  6. 5th Level - F3 & F10
  7. [Rewards: 50,000 vis & 1x Lvl 90 (85,000 Exp) Card] *Note: Only one of the locations in brackets () is correct. I'll edit this once i've solid confirmation*
  8. Gurtrude asks you to look for Brunie. Speak to her in Reboldoeux, she will ask for 5,000 vis from you. Then talk to the old man (Eusibo?) in front of the Auction Lady in Reboldoeux. You need to collect 20 Bulk of Gold, which can be gotten off mobs in Jaquin Prison.
  9. Next, talk to Domingo.
  10. Return to Gurtrude to receive your Gurtrude NPC Card.
  11. (Credit goes to guiltycross)
