Quests - Level 30

Level 30

Quest: The Lost Drawing
NPC + Location: Idge, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: EXP Cards
Difficulty: easy but long irritating
1. Talk to Idge
2. Talk to the investigation team, near Jin Hee near the Pioneering Support Office in Reboldeaux.
3. Talk to Idge.
4. Talk to Jean-Pierre at City of Auch
5. You will be teleported to the market street.
6. Talk to Idge
7. Walk behind here to see the following text: Many footprints Found!
8. Talk to Claude. You will be teleported to an instance to duel him
9. Walk behind him to see the following text: Found a Cottage Key
10. Talk to the other NPCs.
11. Last talk to the item dealer. You will be teleported back to town.
12. Talk to the investigation team.
13. Go to the Tetra Corridor and find a piece of rock at I-J, 8-9.
14. You will be teleported to an instance to battle the thief.
15. Return to Idge to complete the quest.

 Quest: Bernelli's Challenge
NPC + Location: Bernelli, Port of Coimbra
Reward: None
Difficulty: easy till moderate.
1. Talk to Bernelli
2. You will be teleported to an instance to duel her.
3. After you defeat her talk to her to open the quest Suspicion of Tetra Ruins.
Tips: Just stick to the all old tactic of using knock-back skills. Again, I preferred my fighter to have the hack and slash stance on. But, now use shooting star and follow with the first skills. Keep her down and she is an easy kill. (Note: take some SP potions).

Quest: The Suspicion of Tetra Ruins
NPC + Location: Bernelli, Port of Coimbra
Reward: EXP Cards
Difficulty: easy
1. Talk to Bernelli
2. Travel to the door of Tetra Golden Road.
3. Defeat the Treasure Golem.
4. Return to Bernelli to complete the quest
Tips: Try to attack the Golem with skills without a casting time. For example, your fighter's skills work well. When he does his stomp skill, run out of the range. Afterwards, go back in again. A good way to gain SP for your fighter is to fight the normal creeps and then use your best skill on the Golem again. this fight won't be any problem.

Quest: The Sorceress Cherlyn
NPC + Location: Bernelli, Port of Coimbra
Reward: EXP Cards and Grace Bernelli Card
Difficulty: moderate
1. Complete the above quest first.
2. Travel to tetra golden road again.
3. Her you will fight the Treasure golem en the Sorceress Cherlyn.
4. Defeat both and return to Bernelli to complete the quest.
Tips: When you enter the room, walk up north. You will first see the Treasure Golem. Try to stay in the south part of this room. That way the Sorceress Cherlyn does not see you and won't attack you. After clearing the Treasure Golem with them saem strategy as the quest above this, you can start to finish off the Sorceress. Let Bernelli attack her first. That way she won't even lay a finger on you, but only attack Bernelli. just use you best skills and she will die soon enough. In like 1 minute. Quest done
