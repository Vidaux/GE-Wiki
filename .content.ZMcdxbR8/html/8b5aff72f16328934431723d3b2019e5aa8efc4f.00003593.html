Pose Guides

Sword of the New World Pose Guides are books that can be purchased from Andre in the Cite de Reboldoeux. Once a player has read the guide, they can open their pose list (Alt-X), and strike a pose they select from the list; before you can take a pose you must take Auto Defend mode off via the options menu (Alt-O), and then you must knock your characters out of defend by moving them. For more information on what new poses can be learned, and what they look like, click on the links below.
