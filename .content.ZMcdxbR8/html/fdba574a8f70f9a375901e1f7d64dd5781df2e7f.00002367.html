Stances:
 Knuckle - Bare Knuckle

Chapter of Wind
Comments:
 Gracielo can do a lot of damage with just his feet, he's usually used by lower levels to support their team.

How to get:
Suggested level: Around lvl 40 (Do able at level 26+ if you can handle it)
  1. Talk to Gracielo. He will ask you to give him Ferruccio Bread, Milk & Chocolate. Bread and milk can be purchased from the girl at Queen's Gate. Chocolate can be purchased from Lisa Lynway.
  2. Once done, Gracielo will ask you to speak to Camille, here. She will ask you to fight Gracielo (lvl 40).
  3. Return to Gracielo to receive your Gracielo NPC card.


How to get Book of Wind:
Gracielo's Level requirement - 40
*Note: In the following parts, ONLY Gracielo can participate in the mission battles. You will not be able to control ur other 2 characters during instance battles. So it is recommended that you stock up lots of HP pots for Gracielo to use. Any buffs you give Gracielo while in the staging area before the fight will be removed when he zones for his solo fight, though it might be possible for him to use Mitridart (Intensify) and Analeptic remedy (Acceleration) for a lvl 5 buff. I would also recommend placing the HP potions into either the 7th or 8th slot of the skill bar to allow for easier use of them while in combat.*
  1. Gracielo must be in your 3-men team. Set Gracielo as leader and go to the highest point in Coimbra. 3 Thugs will appear and Gracielo has to defeat them.
  2. Talk to Camille (F11), then go to Reboldoeux (H7) and speak to Menendez, the Head of the Combat Squad of the Reboldoeux Guards. You will then battle 100 Reboldoeux Soldiers. They will come in groups of 1, followed by groups of 2, followed by groups of 3, and towards the end of your 100 soldiers they will come in groups of 4.
  3. [Rewards: Lvl 48 Leg Guard, 3x Lvl 40 (19,400 Exp) Cards & 8,000 House Gate points]

Gracielo's Level requirement - 42
  1. Menendez will tell you to challenge the Combat Squad of the Coimbran Guard (C8). Talk to Coimbra's Combat Master. You will have to defeat 4 Coimbran Guards & Hippolite. Hippolite, who is Camille's brother, dies after the battle.
  2. Talk to Camille. She does not accept your excuses for killing Hippolite. You will be teleported to an instance area to break boxes and barrels (Yes. Nothing to kill... just break the boxes)
  3. Next, go back to the highest point of Coimbra. You will battle with the 3 Thugs again. Upon defeating them, 5 Reboldoeux Guards will appear to seek revenge for Hippolite. Defeat the Guards.
  4. Talk to the Substitute Combat Master of Coimbra (C8). You will be teleported. Click on the Tomb of Hippolite. Fritz will appear and tells you to go find a hut located at Porto Bello, A Deserted Quay (G6).

Gracielo's Level requirement - 46
*Note: For the subsequent missions located at the Hut, there are barrels by the side of the hut which can be broken during battles for temporary atk/def/spd bonuses*
  1. Travel to Porto Bello, A Deserted Quay and click on the Door of the Hut, located at G6. Fritz will appear and tells Gracielo to begin his training. Talk to the Scarecrow beside the hut. Gracielo will begin his session of training killing Cannon Tortoises. Kill the of Level 35 Forsaken Cannon Tortoises until they stop spawning. Afterwards, Punch the stone. Gracielo will be angry that he cant break it. You will be warped back to Porto Bello, A Deserted Quay.
  2. Talk to the Scarecrow again to begin the second part of the quest. Kill the Level 35 Forsaken Gray Wolves until they stop spawning. You will be warped back to Porto Bello, A Deserted Quay.
  3. Talk to the Scarecrow again. On the third part of training, Gracielo attempts to break the stone again. Afterwards, talk to the Scarecrow again. Gracielo will battle against a Level 40 Grizzly Scavanger(Pots are recommended at this point). Defeat the beast and you will be able to break the Stone. You will fight a pack of Wolves again, but this time they won't attack you. You will be warped back to Porto Bello, A Deserted Quay.
  4. Fritz will tell you to go challenge his 3 disciples.
  5. Look for LasCasas (G2), in Coimbra, near the warp point. Gracielo will have to fight him(Bring Pots in case).
  6. [Rewards: Secret Skill Book ~Crazy Rush~ & 3x Lvl 40 (25,000 Exp) Cards]
  7. Next, Talk to Reen, the female soldier standing in the center of Reboldoeux Stone Pit (H8). Gracielo will have to defeat her(Bring Pots in case).
  8. [Rewards: Secret Skill Book ~Tornado Vortex~, 3x Lvl 40 (25,000 Exp) Cards & 20,000 House Gate points]
  9. Then, Look for a Worker near the giant stone statue in Ferruccio Junction (F6). Gracielo will have to defeat him(Bring pots in case).
  10. [Rewards: Secret Skill Book ~Dragon Kick~, 3x (27,000 Exp) Cards, 2x Health-fillers & 25,000 House Gate points]
  11. Return to the hut at Deserted Quay and click on the Door. Gracielo will have a talk with Fritz.
  12. [Rewards: Chapter of Wind skillbook, level 40 polish]

Quest Tips
  1. For the fight, take a fighter and block, then use any skill that could make Gracielo fall to the floor. (I used Strike Back - Wizard skill, while the fighter blocked).
  2. Purchasing Gracielo on the market will be unable to do the quest for the stance book (assuming that you already made a Gracielo). You can buy the stance book on the market though.
  3. Sometimes Gracielo will go for your scout instead of your fighter (if your team consists of a scout and a fighter). If it does, keep your scout on auto defend with no weapon so it continually heals itself, then when Gracielo gets near your scout, cast a knockbak spell such as Fireball (Elementalist) or Strike Back (Wizard) and repeat this to win. I did this at around lvl 26 so it just takes some practice to get the timing down but it should be no problem, as long as you have a tank that can take a few of Gracielo's kicks.

Note:
  1. The quest can be completed before lvl 40, and the book quest can completed by level 44. The level requirements are there to give you a level range.
  2. His quests for Chapter of Wind are doable at lvl 40, mine was 41 when i managed to win against Selden -Darkvind
