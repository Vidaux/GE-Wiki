    Viron Quests

    Prerequisites: 5 Elemental Otite quest complete, Family level 20+

    Red Sunset Forest

    Name    : [Viron] Island that was once called the end of the world (1-4)
    NPC: Vespanola Navy Officer
    Location: Viron
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Go to Port Coimbra.
    2. Speak to Captain Ricardo (K6) and set sail to Viron to start this quest.
    3. Talk to the Vespanola Navy Officer (F10).
    3. Report to Lionel (F5).
    4. Report to Farrel (C7).
    5. Talk to Lionel to gain permission to go outside the city. (Must have Letter of Recommendation from Lorch Fruholen).

    Name: [Viron] To the Red Forest (1) -optional-
    NPC: Farrel, Commander in Chief
    Location: Viron
    Prerequisite: completion of quest above
    Reward: None
    Details:
    **This quest and the following are optional to gaining entrance to the Red Sunset Forest Map. (read below)
    *** Completion of this quest is required for Raven and Lionel's recruitment quest.

    1. Talk to Farrel who may help you meet Raven the butler from Fe Lomme Castle.
    2. You are given several choices to bribe Farrel with:
    • 5 Sun Stones
    • 5 Moon Stones
    • 1 Great Stones
    • 1 Elemental Jewels
    • Nothing
    The correct ones are: 1 Great Stones (cash shop), 1 Elemental Jewels, or Nothing (free).
    If you choose the free option, you will have to do the quest below.
    You can give Farrel the Great Stones Or Elemental Jewels at any time you are doing the free option.

    Name: Ridiculous Farrel (1-2) [free option] -optional-
    NPC: Wheelton, Leader of Viron Militia
    Location: Viron
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Talk to Wheelton.
    2. Go to Glacial Fortress: Forecourt to collect:
    • 100 [Perfect Shiny Crystal] from Arctic Rufus Reapers (quest item).
    3. Return to Wheelton.

    Name: [Viron] Red Forest (2) -optional-
    NPC: Lionel
    Location: Viron
    Reward: Red Sunset Pass, Veteran G EXP Card (3), Honor Card B, Shiny Crystal Chest.
    Details:
    1. After bribe Farrel or completion of the free option, talk to Lionel.

    Name: [Viron] Red Forest (3) -optional-
    NPC: Farrel
    Location: Viron
    Reward: Veteran G EXP Card (3), Honor Card B, Shiny Crystal Chest.
    Details:
    1. Talk to Farrel.

    ** If you're not interested in getting Raven or Lionel, you can skip the above optional quests and enter the Red Sunset Forest map another way.
    -Talk to the Vespanola Navy Entrance Manager (E-5) for another way to exit Viron.
    -Give him 3x [White Gold Bar].
    (You can save a warp location when you reach the Red Sunset Forest).

    The Baron Dominion Family

    Name: [Viron] Baron Dominion Family (1-13)
    Location: Viron
    NPC: Baroness Dominion
    Reward: Honor Card B, Honor Card A, Damaged Ancient Polish (2)
    Details:
    1. Talk to Wheelton (I-7) to start this quest chain.
    2. Talk to the Baroness (C-7).
    3. Return to Wheelton.
    4. Talk to the Baroness.
    5. Talk to Dean (G-8).
    6. Talk to Mela (E-8).
    7. Talk to Genia (F-6) to persuade Mela to see the true identity of Dean.
    8. Talk to the Bandelier (G-5) to start an instanced combat "Backstreet Battle".
    9. Talk to the Bandelier again.
    10. Go to H-2 to find the important document that proves Deans true identity.
    11. Talk to Mela and prepare for an instanced combat "His True Identity".
    12. Kill all Mamons and Ruffians in the instance.
    13. Talk to Wheelton.
    14. Talk to Farrel (C-7) and threaten him with the evidence.
    15. Talk to Baroness for reward.
    16. Talk to Wheelton for reward.


    The Devil and the Alchemist

    Name: The Devil and the Alchemist: Genia's Favour
    Location: Viron, Red Forest Sunset
    Prerequisite: Completion of Baron Dominion Family questline
    NPC: Genia
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Quest mail is triggered automatically after completing Baron Dominion Family.
    2. Go to Red Sunset Forest.
    3. Kill Vordoi for 10x [Vordoi's Broken Tooth] (Quest item).
    4. Return to Genia, show her the items and accept the next quest.

    Name: The Devil and the Alchemist: Genia's Research
    Location: Viron, Red Forest Sunset, Blood Fog Forest
    NPC: Genia
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Go to Red Sunset Forest.
    2. Kill Reapers Garas for 5x [Bloodstained Claw] (Quest item).
    3. Go to Blood Fog Forest.
    4. Kill Scissors Garas for 5x [Garas Wet Feather] (Quest item).
    5. Return to Genia and give her the quest items and accept the next quest.

    Name: The Devil and the Alchemist: Genia's Assignment (1)
    Location: Viron, Crow Forest
    NPC: Genia
    Reward: Earth Stone
    Details:
    1. Go to Crow Forest.
    2. Upon entering, you will be warped to a combat instance "Crow Forest".
    3. Defeat the Red Haired Fighter to 1/4 HP to proceed.

    2. Hunt Bow Garas for 10x [Garas Rusty Arrowhead] (quest item).
    3. Return to Genia.

    Name: The Devil and the Alchemist: Genia's Assignment (2)
    Location: Viron, Red Sunset Forest
    NPC: Genia
    Reward: Earth Stone
    Details:
    1. Go to Red Sunset Forest.
    2. Kill Fordoi for 10x [Fordoi’s Broken Nail] (quest item).
    3. Return to Genia.

    Name: The Devil and the Alchemist: The Devil’s Symbol
    Location: Viron, Crow Forest
    NPC: Genia
    Reward: Piece of Naraka, Ability to use Genia’s Enchantment Services, Devil’s Seal Repeatable Quest, Alchemy with 7 Pieces of Naraka
    Details:
    1. Go to Crow Forest.
    2. Kill Gluttony for 1x [Gluttony One Arm] (quest item).
    3. Return to Genia for reward.
    4. Continue onto the next quest "Lionel and the Militia".

    Name: Devil’s Seal (Repeatable Quest)
    Location: Viron, Crow Forest
    NPC: Genia
    Reward:
    If successful: Piece of Naraka - Ma; Mo; Lu; Beel; Al; Than; He.
    If unsuccessful: 3x Sun Stone
    Details:
    1. Talk to Genia and select Devil’s Seal.
    2. Genia will request you to hunt for any one of these quest items from the Viron Forest Maps:
    • 10 x [Vordoi's Tooth]
    • 10 x [Fordoi Claw]
    • 10 x [Garas Claw]
    • 10 x [Garas Feather]
    • 10 x [Arrow Head]
    • 10 x [Gluttony Tongue]
    3. Return to Genia for reward.
    There is no guarantee to successfully gain the reward: Piece of Naraka - Ma; Mo; Lu; Beel; Al; Than; He.
    (Unless you choose Lionel's Side in the Black and White Quest).


    Lionel and the Militia

    Name: Lionel and the Militia: Lionel’s Call
    Location: Viron
    Prerequisite: Completed “The Devil and The Alchemist: The Devil’s Symbol” quest
    NPC: Lionel
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Warp out of Viron and back in to get quest mail.
    2. Talk to Lionel.

    Name: Lionel and the Militia: Lionel’s Request (1)
    Location: Viron
    NPC: Wheelton, Leader of Viron Militia
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Talk to Wheelton.

    Name: Lionel and the Militia: Lionel’s Request (2)
    Location: Viron
    NPC: Lionel
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Talk to Lionel.

    Name: Lionel and the Militia: Viron Security
    Location: Viron
    NPC: Lionel
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Go towards the Vegetable Merchant (E-10).
    2. Enter the instanced combat "Fight at the Fish Market".
    2. Defeat the Mamons, Ruffians, and Bandlier the Taikbin.
    3. After the mission you are given two quest options (Black and White - Justice or Darkness).

    Black and White

    A. Name: Black and White: The Envoy of Justice
    Location: Viron
    Prerequisite: Completed “Lionel and the Militia: Viron Security”
    NPC: Lionel
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Talk to Lionel and select [We’ll Do It].

    Name: Black and White: Devil’s Seal
    Location: Viron
    Prerequisite: Talked to Lionel and selected [We’ll Do It].
    NPC: Genia
    Reward: Devil’s Seal (repeatable quest) reward rate is 100% every time.
    Details:
    1. Talk to Genia.

    OR

    B. Name: Black and White: Temptation of the Darkness
    Location: Viron
    Prerequisite: Completed “Lionel and the Militia: Viron Security”
    NPC: Dean
    Reward: None
    Details:
    1. Talk to Dean and select [We’ll Do It].

    Name: Black and White: Farrel's Suggestion
    Location: Viron
    Prerequisite: talked to Dean and selected [I'll meet him].
    NPC: Farrel
    Reward: Temptation of Darkness quest (repeatable 1x/week)
    Details:
    1. Talk to Farrel.

    Name: Temptation of Darkness: Soul crystal (repeatable)
    Location: Viron
    NPC: Farrel
    Reward: Promotion Recipe - Stance
    Details:
    1. Talk to Farrel.
    2. Go to Glacial Fortress: Forecourt.
    3. Hunt Arctic Rufus Reaper for 50x [Bright Crystal] (quest item).
    4. Return to Farrel on a Sunday for reward.
