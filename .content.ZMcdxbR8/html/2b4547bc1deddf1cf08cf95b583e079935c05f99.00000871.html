This is a list of the back costumes to be found in the game. All timed back costumes have bonus stats. The permanent versions are blank but can be used in a recipe to make a 7 day or 15 day version with stats.stat. Permanent versions are commonly dropped from Karjalain Boxes. These can be worn by any Stock Character or UPC. All back costumes are non-tradeable. Click the icon to see a full image.
