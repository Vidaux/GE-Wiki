Level 20

Quest: Andre's Gone Crazy!!
NPC + Location: Andre Janzur, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: White Robe
Difficulty: easy
1. After completing the Secret Behind the Holy Water. Talk to Andre.
2. You will be teleported to an instance to duel Andre.
3. After defeating him talk to him to complete the quest.
Tips: Same tactic as other rnpcrupc duals. Just use knockdown skills on him. I prefer the first skill of hack and slash. (Note buy some sp potions)

Quest: Sympathy for Dilos Lantemn
NPC + Location: Andre Janzur, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: EXP Cards
Difficulty: easy
1. Talk to Andre Janzur.
2. Go to Al Quelt Moreza Arcade and walk to the tomb around G4 (the tomb looks more like an altar, just walk to it)
3. You will be teleported to an instance. Destroy the 3 portals
4. Return to Andre Janzur to complete the quest

Quest: Man in Despair
NPC + Location: Andre Janzur, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: Irolina Hat
Difficulty: easy
1. After you finish Sympathy for Dilos Lantemn. Talk to Andre Janzur.
2. Talk to Panfilo de Narvaez
3. Complete the quest Pure White Food
4. After you finish the quest Pure White Food take the Carbonara to Andre Janzur and complete the quest.

Quest: Pure White Food
NPC + Location: Panfilo de Narvaez, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: Carbonara
Difficulty: easy
1. Talk to Panfilo de Narvaez
2. Buy the milk from the girl at Queen's Gate.
3. Buy pasta and parmesan from Camille the Grocer at Combria.
4. Kill the boars meat at Cathari Falls to obtain the meat
4. Return to Panfilo to complete the quest.

Quest: Grudge from the Past
NPC + Location: Man, Port of Coimbra
Reward: Deigo's Great Sword, EXP Cards
Difficulty: moderate till irritating
1. Talk to Old Man in the South East section of Coimbra
2. Return to DiegoYaganeh in Reboldoeux.
3. You will be teleported to an instance to duel diego.Yaganeh.
4. Talk to DiegoYaganeh to receive his items.
5. Return to the man at Coimbra to complete the quest
Tips: Stick with the all old tactics of knock-down skills and you should be fine. Maybe level up a bit before attempt this quest. (note: don’t forget to bring some sp potions)
