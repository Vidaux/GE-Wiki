Shotguns Special Shotguns Controllers Knuckles Gaiters Cannons Hammers

Shotgun's Used By :
- Grace Bernelli
- Lorch Feurholden
Note: Shotguns have the natural ability to do AOE damage.

Special Shotgun's Used By:
- Lorch Feurholden
Special Shotguns consist of shotguns with the default ability to sustain fear or stun into the enemy, you can also chip these items to add extra stats without damaging the default ability. Special Shotguns are currently only used by Lorch.

Controller's Used By:
- Catherine the Summoner

At level 84 you can purchase the first controller. At 88 you can choose from two types of controller
1. To increase the summon ATK rating
2. To increase the summon DEF Rating
Note: The controllers can be chipped and has the chance of modding with increased Summon ATK/DEF Rating depending on the type of controller being chipped, however the usual mod outcome is increased SP.

Knuckle's Used By:
- Soso
- Irawain
- Gracielo
- Baek-Ho
- Getrude
Note: All Martial Artist's will be able to equip Knuckles, currently no stock chars can.

Gaiters Used By:
- Soso
- Irawain
- Gracielo
- Baek-Ho
- Soho, The Fighter (Promo UPC)
Note: Gaiters are faster attack speed than Knuckles but loose out on power.

Cannon's Used By:
- Jose
- Claire

Note: - Cannon's have the natural ability to knock back.
- Jose at veteran gets an auto attack cannon stance.

Hammer's Used By:
- Angie
- Jack
- Yeganeh
