    Since the old guide is outdated (it was made for SOTNW in 2007),2010, I thought it was about time to share the secrets to making vis in the current version of SOTNW.Granado Espada

    The Best way for get vis,is the Clock Tower path!

    1. Daily Instanced Raids

    • Castilla Mines
      Drops: Enchantment Chips Lv 100 and Veteran, Ancient Relic Piece, Gold Relic Piece, roulette.
      How to get there: talk to Castilla Exploration Recruit Soldier in Port of Coimbra. Enter the mine at H-3.
      Recommended level: Expert+ if you want to do it fast.

      Most people solo Castilla Mines up until Basilisk (OHKO's at 10% HP left; unless you can freeze/stun it).
      Also killing the normal monsters will yield quite a few "Ancient Relic Piece".
      You can enter the mines for free once each day (resets at 12am SST). You can re-enter the mines more than once a day and keep farming for Enchantment Chips but it will require "Ancient Relic" upon entry. You can get Ancient Relic by exchanging 3x "Ancient Relic Piece" and 3,000 Feso in the Relic Exchanger (E-2 Castilla Garrison).

      The Relic Exchanger is also a great way to try your luck at gaining rare items like the Dragon Heart Recipe, Necklace Recipes and so on. So be sure to collect Ancient Relic Pieces whenever possible!


    • Dr. Torsche's Hidden Basement
      Drops: Enchantment Chip Veteran, Gold Bar, Roulette.

    • Bounty Hunter Guild
      Drops: Enchantment chips, Roulette.

    • Instanced Rafflesia
      Drops: Level 100 gears, Necklace recipes, Elite 84 Armors, Chance of Constellation Box.

    • Instanced Sharffenberger
      Drops: Level 100 gears, Necklace recipes, Elite Schvarlier Piece, Hilt of Elite Weapons, Chance of Constellation Box.


    2. Use a Pet
    If you buy some gold and don't want to waste it on selling cash shop items for fast vis, then do the next best thing: buy feso, get a pet, and use it when you AFK level.

    Great places to AFK (in no particular order):
    • Citadel of the Gods (Caebolan SA/AA) - Red/green/blue rough-stone, Magic-spelled Amethyst, Liquid Gas, Veteran Gloves and Boots [cash shop map]
    • Bahamar Ancient Cave - Snail Shell, Old Chess Piece, Red/green/blue rough-stone, Level 100 gear [cash shop map]
    • Skeleton Ancient Area - Red/green/blue rough-stone [cash shop map]
    • Katovic Snowfield - Cabbage, Golden Apple, Mega Ionium, Outlaw's Cigar (alchemy rating 3), Peace Insignia (alchemy rating 3), A Marksman's Magic Bullet (alchemy rating 3)
    • Errac Tierra, Dias or Noche - Rhodolite, Ancient Rune - Te, Ti, Ar, An
    • Viron, Red Sunset Forest - Letizia Shiny Box, Piece of Naraka - Ma, Mo, Lu, Beel, Al, Than, He.
    • Rion Dungeon Corridor - Shiny Crystal
    • Prison de Joaquin, Torture Chamber - Shiny Crystal
    • Ustiur, Zona Uno - Red/green/yellow Leaf of Rafflesia, Dragon Leather, Shiny Crystal
    • Dr. Torshe's Mansion - Catherine body parts, Strange items (alchemy rating 2), Otite Pieces
    • Viron Forest Maps - Letizia Shiny Boxes,Devils Dreams
    These common loots you acquire by AFKing are very valuable:
    • Level 80-92 gears - convert into chips with the Chip Exchanger to sell on the market.
    • Level 100 gears - break into chips, if it doesn't break at +6, go make a Level 32 crystal at El Dorado and sell.
    • Shiny Crystals - needed for a lot of things now: keys, Bounty Hunter missions, socketing, crafting.
    • Rough-stones - drag them into the Jewel Crafter. Then try your luck at the Jewel Exchanger and hope for a High Quality Spoumene and High Quality Obsidian. (Most people like to exchange jewels that come from blue or green rough-stones).
    You can make tons of vis this way if you are a dedicated AFK leveler.

    3. Clan Raid Missions
    These missions are known as:
    • Sharffenberger the Cursed
    • Mad Rafflesia
    • Mad Uraeus
    • Mad Griffon
    • Madness Farm
    • Curse of the Red Sunset Forest
    These missions are instanced and done once per week. Each mission will give Reward box roulette(s) during or at the end of the mission. There is a lot of potential to get Dragon Heart Recipes, Elite Le Blanc Recipes, Constellation Symbol Boxes, and Pieces of Naraka (which are needed for Stratavista Demon series weapons - 35 AR base). This of course requires you to join a clan (a fairly active or large clan will be helpful in completing these missions).

    4. Alejandro's Boat Quest
    If you have completed the pequod boat quest (Viron) you can speak to the Wharf Manager (E-9) and request to support local or foreign merchants.
    In this trade you will need Pure Talt/Etretanium/Ionium/Quartz/Aidanium.
    You can trade either with Home Merchants (more costly but chance for greater rewards) or Foreign Merchants (less costly but worse rewards).
    Always choose to trade with Home Merchants!
    Possible Rewards: Vis (from 1-5 mill) or Shiny Crystal Box (from 1-9 boxes and each can yield 1-500 Shiny Crystals).


    5. Sell Cash Shop items
    Sword 2 may be Free2Play but if you really want an advantage, you're going to have to spend some money on this game. The best items to sell depend on the server you're playing on and the economy at the time. They will fluctuate. But as a general rule, the most popular items to sell are enchant boosters, upgrade accelerators, soul crystals, and mysterious powders. Sometimes Lacquers may be worth selling. With the new update Mercenary Warrants are no longer tradeable. Avoid trying to sell the lesser used cash shop items like Vet Memoirs unless you know there is a buyer who is looking for it.

    6. Buy Low and Sell High on the Marketplace
    If you see people selling in-demand items for cheap, buy them all! Then resell them. This is a basic money making strategy that exists in life.

    These are the four guaranteed vis making strategies. Two of them require you to be a paying customer. The other two can be done relatively easy now, as the economy has some what dumped recently. Things are selling on the Market Manager for way cheaper than before. To buy low and sell high also requires patients, but remember, if done correctly this can net you around 30mil a week. You do not need to spend real money to get far in SotNW anymore, as cash shop items have become cheap, and easily accessible. Some items will help you get to Master a lot faster than normal, but if you are willing to invest more than a month in Sword 2, you will be able to have multiple vets and experts in a reasonable time.

    7. Test Your Luck
    If a certain item for example a 92e armor will sell for 50m normally but will sell for 150m with 3dr, you could try to enchant it to test your luck. Weapons are typically easier to enchant due to the different stats you could potentially go for such as racial mods. In servers where the enchantment chip prices are low, it is less risky.

    8. Tiburon Game
    With chips worth exponentially higher at the next tier such as 88-92 and 96-100 you could make a profit gambling them with Tiburon. Since 88 will degrade to 84 and 96 will degrade to 92, you will lose the least amount of profit if a gamble goes wrong. Of course it depends on the market prices. But remember, it is possible to completely loose the chips, be sure to look up a Tiburon game faq or guide, as this will help you avoid loosing precious chips.
