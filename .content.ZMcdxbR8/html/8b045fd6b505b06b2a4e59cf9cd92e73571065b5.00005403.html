(also called Diego)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Character Level: 24
Location: Reboldoeux
Job: Ex-Criminal
Armor: Leather Armor
Weapon Usage: Swords
Status:
Str Agi HP Dex Int Cha
4 9 4 7 3 3

Stances:
  1. No Weapon - Install Trap, Structure
  • 2. BuildSword - Back Guard
  • 3. 2 Sword - Hack and Slash

  • Comments: Yeganeh made many mistakes and his crimes ruined many lives. Though, he'd like to make amend, and tries to help people. Very close to Jack type of character, Yeganeh has a different building stance, and can also use swords.

    How to get him:
    Level requirement - 17
    1. Talk to Yaganeh. He will ask you to put up 2 posters, 1 each at the bulletin boards in Reboldoeux and Coimbra. Return to him when done.
    2. [Rewards: 3x Lvl 1 (4,000 Exp) Cards]

    Level requirement - 20
    1. Talk to the Old Man in Coimbra, found close the the bridge near M'boma. He will ask you to fight Yaganeh (lvl 25). Return to the Old Man once you have defeated Yaganeh.
    2. [Rewards: Yaganeh's Sword (lvl 24) & 3x Lvl 20 (7,840 Exp) Cards]

    Level requirement - 25
    1. Talk to Yaganeh again. He will ask you to look for Jack at Reboldoeux Stone Pit to fix his tool. Once done, go back to Diego.
    2. [Rewards: 3x(7,840 Exp) Cards]
    3. Next, Yaganeh will ask you to hunt 20 Jumeau plaque Rouge (lvl 22), 20 Jumeau plaque Noir (lvl 23) & 25 Phobitan Imps (lvl 23) at Tetra Hills.
    4. [Rewards: 3x Lvl 20 (7,840 Exp) Cards]

    Level requirement - 28
    1. This time, Yaganeh will ask you to collect 15 Persistent Rubber and 15 Steel Iron. Travel to Tetra Great Ruins to hunt Phobitan Rouges (lvl 26).
    2. [Rewards: 3x Lvl 20 (9,000 Exp) Cards, 1xHealing Ampule]
    3. Now, Yaganeh will ask you to get a licence from Domingo in Reboldoeux and Nunez in Coimbra. Speak to Domingo to get Reboldoeux's Licence. Next, Speak to Nunez in Coimbra. He will ask you to hunt 5 Jumeau plaque Eton (lvl 24) at Tetra Great Ruins. Return to Nunez to receive Coimbra's Licence.
    4. Return to Yaganeh to receive your UPC Card & 3x Lvl 20 (9,000 Exp) Cards.

    -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    Quest Tips
    1. For the battle, make sure you have a good bloker, or else his fast throwing knives will one hit all of your characters. Avoid those attacks and maintain Yaganeh in the floor.
