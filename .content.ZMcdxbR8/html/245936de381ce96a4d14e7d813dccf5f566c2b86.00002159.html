    Game Bosses

    About This Section:
    In this section you will find information pertaining to the bosses found throughout the game. Bosses have better stats than their normal monster counterparts. They have a much higher attack rating, attack damage, defense rating, defense, elemental and mental resists and loads of health (hit points). Bosses will come in 3 main types; zone, raid, and battle arena. The links below list the racial modifier types of each boss. Having weapons modded to these racial types will increase the amount of damage you will do to them. Some bosses can be killed by a single family, whereas others require squads of multiple families.

    Some raid bosses may be missing from this section. Information on them can be found in the Raids section of the wiki.
