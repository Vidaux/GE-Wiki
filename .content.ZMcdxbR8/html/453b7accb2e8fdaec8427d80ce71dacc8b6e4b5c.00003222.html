    Murphy's Laws of the New World

    There are some things that just can't be avoided...

     Some of the Pioneers noticed that there were some forces that they couldn't control in the new world, they called those "Laws" and were writen by Murphylus Hazzard. This is the most up to date list; if you come up with a law that you know must be writen here, post it in a thread and we'll add it.

     Murphy's Laws of the New World
    • Your resser will be amongst the first to die.
    • No matter what, you can't win against lag.
    • The chance of an enchanting rolling blank or useless is directly proportional to the cost of the chip or the desire of patron.
    • Even on the carebear servers deceit, under handiness, bloodlust, and power mongering still reign.
    • Bots on the same router flock together.
    • Just because you reported a bot ages ago, doesn't mean they won't be expert before you.
    • The difficulty of leveling a stance is directly proportional to the level of the stance and the character.
    • The really valuable items don't have a sound effect when they drop.
    • Your last enchant chip will always disappoint you.
    • Shoes tend to enchant with Evasion.
    • 30% of all of the weapon drops are bracelets.
    • 40% of all of the armor drops are robes.
    • During a lobby the highly desired drop(s) usually go to the person that requires it the least, and is the least likely to offer it to those who do.
    • In Colony War permanent alliances, aren't.
    • There's never enough room in your Quarters.
    • There's never enough slots in your Saved Warp List.
    • There's never enough space in your Storage with Leo.
    • You never have enough vis.
    • If you don't pick it up, a bot surely will.
    • If a box spawns near you, there's always someone there to shot it.
    • You tend die on the hardest part of the quest because of the lag monster.
    • You always get something you think as worthless when your low level, but don't get it when your high level.
    • Selling Cash Shop items for vis is always better then giving your credit card to bots.
    • Your characters will sometimes die when you look away for 30 seconds.
    • You'll be able to semi AFK fine all day while at your computer, but die as soon as you go use the bathroom. [Read the following laws as to why / how!]
    • Mobs that can't KO (knockout) your toons when you are active, will gain super powers as soon as you go AFK.
    • The box appears more frequently after you AFK.
    • The boss spawns just after you AFK.
    • The PK approaches soon after you AFK.
    • You'll run out of ammo 3 hours after you AFK.
    • The value loot drops while you are AFK.
    • The value loot vanishes the moment you resume from AFK.
    • Catherine's hip always drops from the first Lloyd after you AFK.
    • Your AFK spot will likely be taken when you leave to resupply, revive, or do a trade. Or even while your still there!
    • While AFK in Rion, the purple cockatrice will always find you!
    • The purple cockatrice of Rion will follow you all around the map without fail, you can run but you just can't hide!
    • Maintenance happens 30 minutes after your ready for a long gaming session.
    • No matter how high you level, there's always another character that needs another.
    • You can spend 20 hours looking for a hip, all you can get is a hand.
    • There are no elites for summoners.
    • No pics, then it didn't happen.
    • The item(s) that you want to buy the most are rarely available for sale at a reasonable price.
    • NPCs, uncontrolled characters, and camera angles rarely cooperates when taking a screen shot or making a video.
    • The vowel O does not exist in the New World, where as G's and E's can be found in the hundreds. How they spell in the New W0rld is a mystery.
    • When asking directions, over here is not over there.
    • Even with 20 Upgrade Accelerators there is no such thing as a 100% chance of success when strengthening an item to +5.
    • You always remember to restock AFTER you enter the 500K section of Fire Isle Zone... This also applies to after you enter the Relics Underground / Sacred Dungeon.
    • The most wanted mods go on random weapons you pick up.
    • If you have kids, they will always crave attention at the moment a Boss spawns or you get mobbed and need to spawn skills.
    • If you have cats, they will always crave attention at the moment a Boss spawns or you get mobbed and need to spawn skills.
    • The recipes/Elites you do find will 50% of the time be the ones you: A ) Already have or don't need. Or B ) Be Cannons/Shotguns.
    • Just because the area says 84+ doesn't mean you will actually FIND any level 84+ loot.
    • Unarmed Scouts when put on Defend Mode, heals you when you don't need it, and doesn't when you're dying.
    • When in tournaments, your scout will never heal itself when it needs to, and will heal itself when it has max HP...
    • Scouts will serve no purpose at higher levels. [Mostly because the ones are present in the higher level, usually end up dead anyways.]
    • When in an area with flying mobs, don't use skills, or else the flying mobs will attack!
    • No matter how much tax money a clan has received, the members will always be so poor that they have to farm weak Bosses for stuff to sell.
    • While you are trying to aggro a raid boss, some n00b will never understand "don't attack until the tank says go."
    • The monster you are looking for will not show up until you don't need to exterminate them anymore.
    • The Drama Llama has many tenders.
    • Exceptions to the Laws are no longer applicable after you begin to rely on those exceptions.
    • When in tournaments, your scout will never heal itself when it needs to, and will heal itself when it has max HP...

    Note: El_Hazard made this list on the official forum, you can     see it here.
