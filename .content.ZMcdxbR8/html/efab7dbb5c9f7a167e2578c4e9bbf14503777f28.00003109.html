Mission Lobby: Highway

Mission: Highway 1

Victory conditions: Shield home of friend's army within the given time limit.
TIP: Shield home of friend's army against Monster while guarding the (x)



What spawns: King's Garden mobs, Tetra Hill mobs
minimum level: 24
__________________________________________________________



Mission: Highway 2

Victory conditions: Shield home of friend's army within the given time limit.
TIP: Shield home of friend's army against Monster while guarding the (x)



What spawns: Porto Bello/Deck(?)/Cabin/Hold/Deserted Quay mobs, Parsiar Captain, Nosair Captain, Wahlansche Boss
minimum level: 60
note: since some of these are flying, be sure to have some ranged attackers
strategy:
  • (by Auxiliadora) "Character lvls 60 atleast with atleast one musk. First u just need to stand near 3rd spawn point and let them destroy the first 2 home blues or atleast the first one. When that is done stand the the middle of the instance.You should see a round circle on the floor and thats where u should stand, and let them all come to you. The mobs only go for the 3rd home blue if all 3 characters are dead.So if atleast one of your characters is alive they will never go for the 3rd home blue. They arent that hard if u got a musk since most of them can be almost 2 shotted with birdfall."
