Character Level: 44 (Level 1 if bought with the game)
Location: Reboldoeux
Job: Gun Merchant
Armor: Leather Armor, CoatsArmor
Weapon Usage: Sabres, Rifles, Bayonets
Status:
Str Agi HP Dex Int Cha
6 7 5 7 3 3

Stances:
  1. Sabre - Middle Guard
  2. 2 Sabre - Twin Blades
  3. Rifle - Aggressive Shot, Inroad Shot, Encounter Shot

    Comments:
    Najib Sharif is gun retailer at Reboldoeux. He travels a lot and is very interested into all kind of treasure. You'll meet him really soon and keep regular contacts with that man during your first days on Vespanolan territory. His ability to use both rifles and sabers can make him deadly at any range.

    How to get:
    First part (Required Level : 1)
    1. You first start out at Pioneering Office and Sir Lyndon asks you to go to Najib Sharif at the market area of Reboldoeux. Do that and talk to Najib Sharif, the Firearm NPC.
    2. Najib then asks you to go talk to Vera about the Oporuto Tea Box (Quest Item Obtained: Oporuto Tea Box)
    3. Go talk to Vera (She is located near Andre)(Cite de Reboldoeux, D9), she'll tell you everything she knows about the Oporuto Tea Box. After the conversation, you now head back to Najib and tell him everything. Najib then gives you some EXP cards. (Expandable Item Obtained: EXP Cards x 3)

    Second Part (Required Level : 12)
    1. After reaching level 12, Najib will ask you to go to Al Quelt Moreza Nartex and hunt for Vednabiums.
    2. Go to Al Quelt Moreza via waypoint and go to Nartex (First level), go hunt Vedanobas and you'll obtain Vednabiums. You need 15 pcs to complete the quest.
    3. Report back to Najib and give him the Vednabiums. In return you'll receive some EXP cards. (Expandable Item Obtained: Exp Cards x 3)

    Third Part (Required Level : 36)
    1. After reaching level 36, Go talk to Najib and he will ask you to get 10 Blazing Rubies from Tetra Golden Road.
    2. You need to fight Reapers in order to get the rubies. Reapers are quite rare in this area and they also seldom drop those rubies.
    3. After obtaining 10 pieces, report back to Najib and he'll give you some EXP cards. (Expandable Item Obtained: Exp Cards x 3)

    Fourth Part (Required Level : 41)
    1. After reaching level 41, Najib will ask you to get 40 minerals from Porto Bello in order for him to make the Barkir Riffle.
    2. Go to Porto Bello or even at Porto Bello Deck if you want to and hunt mermans in order to get the minerals. Get 40 pcs and go back to Reboldoeux
    3. After giving the minerals to Najib, he then makes the Barkir Riffle and he gives it to you. (Expandable Item Obtained: EXP Cards x 3) (Equipment Item Obtained: Barkir Riffle *Lv 44*)

    Fifth Part (Required Level : 50 or 55 (Need confirmation))
    1. After reaching level 50, go talk to Najib, he then asks you to get Sunflower Pigment from the Sunflower Monkeys on Thueringenwald. But in order to fight the leader of the Swamp Monkeys, you need to get 20 pcs of the following Pigments. Pigment Aster, Pigment Hydrangea, Pigment Geranium.
    2. You must first go to Thueringenwald and fight lots of Swamp Monkeys. Believe me, the drop rates of those Pigments are horrible. And also, beware of the Boss Swamp Monkey on the map xD
    3. After gathering all pigments, you then talk to the stone located at this area (Thueringenwald, G7). You'll be engaged a fight with the Sunflower Swamp Monkey.
    4. After defeating the Sunflower Swamp Monkey. You then receive the Sunflower Pigment najib is talking about.
    5. Return to Najib and show him the sunflower pigment. He then gives you EXP cards in return (Expandable Item Obtained: EXP Cards x 3)

    Sixth Part
    1. After the pigments, Talk to Najib, he then asks you to investigate the Old Pot he has. He then asks you to go to Vera (Cite de Reboldoeux, D9) and ask her if she knows anything about it. (Quest Item Obtained: Old Pot)
    2. You'll have a series of conversation with various NPCs starting from Vera.
    3. (Port of Coimbra, H10)
    4. This guy, asks you to get 30 Old Wine from Pasiars at Porto Bello Deck.
    5. Once you got the 30 Old Wine, bring it back to the man who asked for it. Then talk to that woman (Port of Coimbra, I7)
    6. (Quest Item Obtained: Coq au Vin).
    7. Bring the Coq au Vin to Nunez (Port of Coimbra, G4)
    8. (Quest Item Obtained: Cameo).
    9. Go back to the man who asked for Old Wine.
    10. (Quest Item Obtained: Kaleidoscope)
    11. After all the conversations, you then go back to Najib and report your findings about them. Quest done.

    Seventh Part (Required Level : 60)
    1. After reaching level 60, go talk to Najib, this time he asks you to collect 30 Antique Silvercandle and 30 Rococo Furnitures at Torsche Mansion.
    2. You can get Antique Silvercandle from the candle enemies at Torsche Mansion: Savage Garden or at Torsche Mansion: Reception Hall. You get an antique silvercandle for every kill.
    3. You can get Rococo Furnitures at Torsche Mansion: Grand Library, by fighting those tables. Get 30 and you're done
    4. After getting all these items, go back to Najib, he then turns it into a Unique Furniture and asks you to go to Vera and ask her if she knows anything about it (Quest Item Obtained: Unique Furnitures)
    5. Go to vera, and you'll be redirected to Vera's Treasure Room and fight the boss. After doing so, Vera will thank you and you converse with her for a bit.
    6. Go back to Najib and he then gives you some EXP cards (Expandable Item Obtained: EXP Cards x 3)

    Eighth Part
    1. Talk to Najib and he gives you the last quest to get his card (Finally!!). He asks you to go to El Tejado Verde to get 20 Hard Wood and some Demonic Oil. Beware of the Demonic Boss on the map.
    2. Go to El Tejado Verde via Auch and get 20 Hard Woods from the small trees you see on the map. Every kill gives you 1 Hard wood.
    3. Get demonic oils from (I think they are named Demonic =/) Demonics at the map.
    4. After gathering the materials, you then go back to Najib and he then gives you the NPC card Najib Sharif and Exp cards (Expandable Item Obtained: EXP Cards x 3) (Loot Item Obtained: Najib Sharif NPC Card)
