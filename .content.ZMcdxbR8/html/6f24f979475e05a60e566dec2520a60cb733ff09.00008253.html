    • Tora Info part 1 Trigger: Move to Byron.
      Pre-requisite: Family lv20+, Finished Byron-story until Lucifer Castle Entry, Finished Blitz Assault quest (which includes finishing Adelina's and Lisa's extended quests) (Optional: Lucifer Castle entry quest, or pay the guard EJs to advance to the locations)
      Items needed within the quest: 300x Dragonfly Silk, 1000x Ice Crystal, 20x Dried Maroon Grass
      Special note: Lisa has to be included in your team almost all the time while doing this questline. A few points need Adelina (normal) included in team too.
      Move to Byron. Include Lisa in your team (From now and almost all the other steps unless stated otherwise. I will not write "Include Lisa" in every step.) and have her talk to Oswalt at H11.
      Move to Byron - Crow Forest. Go to C4 and try to enter Lucifer Castle. You will be asked if you wish to move to an instance.
      Move to H3, entrance to the Laboratory. A quest-dialouge will trigger.
      Move to D8 in the Laboratory and talk to June.
      Move to J4 in the Laboratory to trigger a quest dialouge.
      Move to E6 in the Basement Warehouse to trigger a quest dialouge. You will be moved to an instance.
      Move to C4 in the Basement Warehouse to trigger a combat instance. Protect the npc's. If (at least the fighter?) they die, you fail. Preferably provoke the mobs away from the fighter and kill them like that. This is tricky and might need several tries. The monsters hurt, alot.

      Move to F8/G8 in Lucifer Secret Garden to trigger an instance.
      Move to F9/G9 in Lucifer Moonlight Garden to trigger an instance.
      Move to Byron and talk to Oswalt.
      Move to Port of Coimbra and talk to Adelina.
      Move to the following locations in Port of Coimbra to trigger some instances: F9/G9 - F7 - F10 - F2.
      Move to K6 at the Nimrod Bridge to trigger an instance.
      With Adelina (normal) in team, move to Castilla, base 1. Talk to Elisa.

    • Tora Info part 2 Collect the following items: 300x Dragonfly Silk (1/21 Bigfoot and 1/10 Elite Bigfoot at Snowfield of the Ice Iwzard), 1000x Ice Crystal (1/13 Arctic Dracane, 1/14 Bigfoot, 1/7 Elite Bigfoot, 1/20 Elviot at Snowfield of the Ice Wizard)) and 20x Dried Maroon Grass (1/75 Eltetah in Katovic Snowfield).
      With Adelina in your team, return to Elisa with the items.
      Include Lisa in your team from now on again and move to F9/G9 in Coimbra to trigger a quest dialouge.
      Move to Byron and talk to Oswalt.
      Move to Coimbra and talk to Adelina.
      With Adelina in your team, move to G2 in Coimbra to trigger an instance.
      With ONLY Lisa (MCC1) in your team, move to K6 at the Nimrod Bridge to trigger a combat instance. Defeat Tora (72 AR/DR 269 DEF 690k HP) using only Lisa. After this instance you will recieve Tora's character card.




      【Dark Avenger】


      Javelin + Dagger
      When you create Tora you will notice that he already has his "expert stance". As far as I know you can use it already at Veteran-level, however the stancerings require Tora to be at least Expert to equip them.
