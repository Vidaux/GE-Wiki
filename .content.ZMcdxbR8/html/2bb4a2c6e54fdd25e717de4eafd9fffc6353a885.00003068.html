Character Level: 56
Location: City of Auch
Job: Item Crafter - Magic Weapon Seller
Armor: Leather,Leather (Generic only), Robe
Weapon Usage: Rapier, ice bracelet
Status:
Str Agi HP Dex Int Cha
6 7 5 5 7 3


Stances:
  1. Epee Guard
  2. Sabre Guard
  3. Epee Glacier (Ice bracelet in offhand. Includes Chilling Touch and Absolute Zero)
  4. Sabre Glacier (Ice bracelet in offhand. Includes Chilling Touch and Frost Cloud)

Comments: You will need a character with an Andre series armor equipped to start the quest. You will also need Andre himself.

Required Level: Any character Level 84 with level 84 Andre Armor. Note that elite armor will not work.

Quests:
Required level: 1 Level 84 char with Level 84 Andre series armor equipped (one of the armors that he sells)
Additional Requirement: You must have Andre in your current group. An Andre of any level is fine.
1. Have the Lv. 84 character be the leader and talk to Karjalain at Auch. You will have a series of conversations with her.
Where can she be found?

City of Auch
2. After the conversation, you need Andre to be the leader and talk to her. Again, they'll have a series of conversations that will trigger a fight with her.
 3. The fight will be her vs. your family, the stats for her during this fight are shown below.

 Name: Karjalain
Classification: Human Armor: Soft
Element: No Attribute Size: Medium
Level: 57 Max HP: 34485
ATK Rating: 28 Fire RES: 0
ATK: 634 Ice RES: 0
DEF Rating: 28 Lightning RES: 0
DEF: 46 Mental RES: 0

4. After the fight, you will have another series of conversations again, and voila, Karjalain's card is now in your hands.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Quest Tips

  1. Don't pass the conversation fast =P Its pretty funny, you HAVE to see it yourself lol.
  2. I had to repeat Step 1 several times for it to work for me. I would recommend setting the level 84+ person with the level 84 Andre armor equipped as leader and don't give up if she doesn't start the quest the first time.
  3. After you talk to Karjalain with the character wearing the 84 Armor, she will talk to you, and go on and on about how she wants to meet Andre, THEN you talk to her with Andre, Any level Andre is fine, Mine was 57, be careful, she can kill Andre at that level, but you dont need him to kill her in the fight. After the fight, Voila, Card is yours! -Savengaard
