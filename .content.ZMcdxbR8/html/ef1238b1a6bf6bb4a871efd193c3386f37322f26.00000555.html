Wiki of the New World


Welcome to Wiki Of The New World, a fan-made wiki of the massively multiplayer online game Granado Espada Online (formerly known as Sword 2 and Sword of the New World: Granado Espada), licensed in North America by T3Fun and Europe by EuroGamez GmbH. Here you'll find info about items, characters, quests, and much more. This wiki, like all wikis, is a nonstop work in progress. We need your help to fill in the wiki. So if you have any information that helps the site, add it. If you find anything about the wiki that you think should be changed but don't have the privileges to change (i.e. page movement), let us know. Ask before posting a link to off-site guides, etc.
