Quests - Level 72

LVL 72

Quest: Birds of a Feather Flock Together
NPC + Location: Andre Janzur, Cite de Reboldoeux
Reward: Andre Janzur's Card, Polish: Explorer x3
1. Talk to Andre
2. Travel to Rion Prarie, Bonavista River and kill Cockatries to receive 150 White feathers (rare drop). The Cockatrices in Rion Dungeon won't drop the feathers.
3. Talk to Andre to complete the quest


 Quest: Viki, the boy of the plateau
NPC + Location: Dr Torsche, Wednesday at Viki's House
Reward:
 Difficulty: Irritating long
1. Travel to Mysterious Hut (Tejado Verde, G3). It must be a Wednesday to start this quest.
2. Talk to Dr Torsche
3. Talk to Viki
4. Talk twice to a boy in Reboldoeux, H6


 Quest: Boy, have a dream
Reward: ROBO R-90
Reward:
 1. Talk to Yeganeh about the Robot R-90
2. Travel to Cathari Falls to hunt Saberboars for 20 Wild Boar's Molar
3. Travel to Jezebel Glen to hunt Crate Octopus for 20 Laminated Jezebelium
4. Travel to Tetra Great Ruins to hunt Phobitan Warrior for 5 Roboten Jewels
5. Talk to Yeganeh in Reboldeaux

 Quest: Raw Taste
NPC+Location: Lisa Lynway, Coimbra
Reward: Cacao Sonador
1. Talk to Lisa
2. Travel to Tera Hills or Vegas Javier to hunt any Phobitan for 300 * Carbosse
3. Talk to the Old Lady Cabosse, Coimbra G10
4. Talk to Lisa (she'll charge you 5,000 vis to make Cacao Sonador)


Quest: Viki, the boy of the plateau (?)
NPC+Location: Viki, Mysterious Hut
Reward: L64 Enhancement Chip and 3x Polish: Explorer
1. Talk to Viki
2. You will be teleported to an instance.
3. Defeat the spiders
4. Defeat Cherlyn and a Treasure Golem (L60, 80000 HP)
5. Talk to Ararat
6. Talk to Viki back in the Mysterious Hut.


Quest: Ararat Repair 1
NPC+Location: Sir Ulrich, Mysterious Hut
Reward: 3x Polish: Explorer
1. Talk to Sir Ulrich and pick the second option to start the next part of the quest, Ararat Repair 1.
2. Travel to Stone Pit to hunt Hornets for 100 Trasynix Jewels
3. Travel to Ferrucio Junction to hunt Larval Dragonwasp for 100 Koenig Jewels
4. Travel to Cathari Falls or King's Garden to hunt Little Comodo for 100 Sterten Chrome
5. Talk to Sir Ulrich again.


Quest: Ararat Repair 2
NPC+Location: Sir Ulrich, Myserious Hut
Reward: 3x Polish: Explorer
1. Talk to Sir Ulrich
2. Travel to Tejado Verde to hunt Lightning Striped Spiders for 100 Molythea Block.
3. Travel to Tetra Hill to hunt Little Plaque Rouge for 100 Fire Cube.
4. Travel to El Lago de Tres Hermanas to hunt Corlien Slashers for 100 Garft Block
5. Talk to Sir Ulrich again to start the next part


Quest: Ararat Repair 3
NPC+Location: Sir Ulrich, Mysterious Hut
Reward: 9x Polish: Explorer, Hill Giant Summon Stone, Amicus Skillbookm Viki Character Card and Old Wooden Pendant.
1. Sir Ulrich will then ask you to find a Mamund and an Enchancement Heart.
2. For the Enchancement heart talk to Dr. Torsche in Dr. Torsche's Laboratory back in his mansion. This will open the quest, ??? (See next quest). [someone please find the name].
3. For the Mamund travel to Pradera de Ceniza to hunt Violent Gehcos. This is the L65 map boss. Mamund is a rare drop so you'll have to kill a few.
4. When you have both items talk to Sir Ulrich.
5. Talk to Viki and you'll be teleported to the instance, "Resurrect. Ararat!".
6. Talk to Ararat and Cherlyn and Hill Giant will appear.
7. Kill them both to finish the instance. Hill Giant is L10 and Cherlyn is L20.
8. Talk to Sir Ulrich to complete the quest


Quest: Torsche's Errand
NPC+Location: Dr. Torsche, Dr. Torsche's Laboratory
Reward: Enhancement Heart, Artificial Stone and 3x Polish: Explorer
1.Hunt for 100 Otite Chips (regular item not quest item dropped by all monsters in the Mansion)
2. Hunt for 20 a Strange Spring
3. Hunt for 20 Strange Tube (Drop by Haunted Armors)
4. Hunt for 20 Strange Cogwheel (Drop by Haunted Candles and Haunted Desk / Furnitures)
5. Hunt for 20 Strange Hinge (Drop by Haunted Desk / Furnitures)
6. Hunt for 20 Strange Clock (Drop by Lloyd and Haunted Book)
7. Talk to Dr Torsche in his Laboratory.


Quest: Get Viki's Testis Skill Book
NPC + Location: scarecrow, ''El cannon de Diabolica''
Reward:
1. Travel to ''El Cannon de Diabolica''
2. Talk to the scare crow with Viki as your leader



3. Select the 2nd option...



Viki will say no worry i will help/protect you all

Cherlyn's Conspiracy
4. You will be teleported to an instance
5. You see 3 mysterious basic character of the NPC. I don't know what they do. Now just wait for the conversation to end.
6. Kill the Treasure Golem
7. Talk to the mysterious circle ray/light
8. Travel to ''El Ruina de Memoria''



9. Walk around the RED CIRCLE [above pic] and it will automatic trigger the quest
10. Choose "Yes, I do"
11. You will be teleported to an instance
12. Kill Cherlyn and the Takion, the Treasure Golem can be left alone.
13. Talk to the mysterious circle ray/light
14. Talk to Ulrik with Viki as your leader to complete the quest

Quest: Return of the Giant - Part 1
NPC + Location: Ulrik, Viki's Cabin
Reward: Treasure Core
Difficulty:
1. Finish the above quest
2. Talk to Ulrik
3. Gather the following materials
200 * copper pieces
200 * Bulks of Gold
200 * Mega Aidaniums
100 * Golden Fruits
4. Talk to Ulrik to complete the quest

Quest: Return of the Giant - Part 2
NPC + Location: Ulrik, Viki's Cabin
Reward: Castis Skill Book, Mustang Core
Difficulty:
1. Talk to Ulrik
2. Gather the following materials
200 * Bulk Coals
200 * Bulk of Silver
60 * Mega Etretanium
50 * Philosopher's Stones
3. Talk to Ulrik with Viki as your leader to complete the quest
