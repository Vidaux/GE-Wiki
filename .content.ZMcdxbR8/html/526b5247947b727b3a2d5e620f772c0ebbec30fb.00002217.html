NOTE: If you have finished this quest or have gotten furthur than I, can you please fill in the missing information.

Garcia's Questline continues on from the Bahamar questline. You must have completed it before Elenore will allow you to start as it follows on in the chain of events. You may also need to have at least started the Katovic Snowfield questline (confirmation please?).



Quest: Joaquin Suppression
NPC+Location: Elenore, Auch
Reward: 3x Event Triumph Fillers, 10x Event Health Regen Potions, 10x Soul Regen Potions and 3x Polish: Veteran.
1. After completing the last Bahamar quest, "Ten Nobles of Vespanola" leave the Auch map and return. The quest "Joaquin Suppression" will automatically appear once you re-enter the map.
Talk to Elenore and she will give you the option to join the suppression force, you apparently cannot have Eduardo on your team when you talk to Elenore or you will not be able to trigger the quest.
2. Upon opting to join, you'll be warped to an instance. Destroy the two colonies in 20 minutes to complete it. You will be helped by Republican Soldiers, Reem and a colony buff of +100% HP and +50% Attack and hindered by L88 Failed Subjects. This is a very easy instance, especially at Vet. Just ignore the Failed Subjects and destroy the colonies. At the end you'll see Garcia and he'll do some talking.
3. Talk to Elenore afterwards to receive your reward.
Joaquin Suppression instance
Failed Subjects ---------------------------------------------------------- First Colony ---------------------------------------------------------- Centre Colony


Quest: Simon Ayende's Call 3
NPC: Simone Ayende, Auch Republican Embassy
Reward: None
1. Simon asks you to find info on Garcia.
2. Go to Ferrucio Wall near the Katovic Snowfield entrance. Please note the monsters at Ferrucio Wall hurt a lot even with Veteran Pioneer armour, especially the Reapers. You'll see a ridge near a camp at F2-F3, walk near there until a message pops up (see image below, Selva is around where the arrow is not the red circle).
3. Select the mission. You'll need to fight a L120 Snow Storm Apparition with Selva and Garcia helping. This is fairly easy.
4. When the Apparition is dead talk to Selva for dialogue and to end the instance and start the next part of the questline.
Selva's Location in Ferrucio Wall (at the red circle, approach the circle from the south)





L120 Snow Storm Apparition


Quest: Ryrin's Heart
NPC+Location: Selva, carried on from previous quest.
Reward: 3x Polish: Veteran
1. You'll be asked to talk to Invierno who is at D7 of Frozen Plain. His location is marked on the area map and in the image below.
2. Selva and Garcia will join in the conversation and you'll be required to collect:
  • 50 Philosophers Stones
  • 50 Ice Crystals
  • 10 Cursed Determinations
Philosopher's Stones and Ice Crystals can be obtained from most monsters in the Katovic Region or can be found on the Market Manager. Cursed Determinations are obtained by killing Snow Storms spawned by dropping Ghost Nukes on Ryrin's Heart. Invierno will give you 10 of these.
3. Hand these items over to Invierno.
Invierno's location in Frozen Plain (D7)




Quest: Snowdown in the Snowfield
Reward: Sage Necklace (3 days) and 3x Polish: Veteran
1. Speak to Invierno and he will send you to the instance, "Showdown in the Snowfield".
2. Here you will battle Montoro (see stats below) with the help of Invierno, Garcia and Selva. If you have at least Veteran Pioneer equips then you should find it easy to beat him. Once you get Montoro down to a quarter health he will run and leave you with 7-8 Frozen Waste monsters to battle instead. Kill them. After they die the trio will talk to you and the instance will be complete.
3. Speak to Invierno afterwards.

NOTE: The Sage Necklace which adds +3 AR and +3 DR has a 3 day timer on it. This timer will start as soon as you equip it. It's a good idea to save this until you're ready to do Novia as you'll be needing it. You'll find it in your Premiums inventory.
Showdown in the Snowfield


Montoro's stats


Sage Necklace
Quest: Seal of Novia
Reward:
1. Enter the Ice Wizard Tower and take a few steps forward to trigger a pop-up message asking if you wish to move to the mission to battle Novia
Once you entered the Ice Wizard Tower, move forward a few steps and a window will trigger asking you if you want to advance to the mission. Selecting yes will warp you to the instanced mission.

Once you’re warped to the mission the battle will start against Novia. This will be no easy battle as you will not only face off Novia, but with her “summons” and Montoro. Selva and Garcia will aid you in the battle.
The AR/DR buff spot

NOTE: It is extremely important you stand at the following spot as indicated if you even want an easier battle. As soon as you are warped to the instance click this point to move. It will give your family the Old Man buff of +5 AR/DR. You may be fighting a quest version of Novia but nevertheless she’s still a tough cookie.

lvl 130 = Novia, lvl 110 = Montoro, lvl 120 = Novia’s summons

You need to take down Novia's summons and bring down Novia’s HP to 50% to complete the quest. Montoro is an optional opponent. There is a video of the fight done by a jGE user with an expert STR Catherine. Here's a link:

 http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C3FCPPe6j7E
*Vital battle tip - Bring Elementalists or rNPCs with the lightning evocation stance; excluding Montoro, the others are very weak to lightning but remember to cover them while they spam away their skills.

After the battle, Garcia has been turned undead and you need to save him.

So talk to Invierno again, and he will tell you that you need 1 soup and 10 philosopher's stones to create a remedy to save Garcia. 1 soup requires 50 Wolf’s meat, 20 Golden apples, 50 cabbages and 50 beets. Find more details on these items at the bottom of the Katovic Snowfield Quest Page

Note: If you have not done the soup quest in the Katovic Snowfield quest section, you will not be able to proceed to this part.

After saving Garcia, depending on what you chose when Simon offered you two choices, Garcia will either join you immediately or he will enter into a battle against you. But either way, you will get Garcia’s card.

Scenario 1

If you chose Simon’s second option, which is to report to him after you have found/caught Garcia, you can get Garcia’s card as your reward by talking to him back at Auch.

Scenario 2

If you chose Simon’s first option, which is to hand him over to the Office of Pioneering Support , you will be engaged into a battle with him.

Garcia’s stats

After you win/lose, Garcia will run off and you will need to find him. If you want to know his location, speak with Tiburon (ugh) and he will only tell you Garcia’s location if you pay him 1,000,000 Vis.

To those who have done Lorch’s quest before, you should know better than to choose the 200,000 Vis offer.

After you found his location, return to converse with Simon Ayende to receive Garcia’s card AND a lvl 100 enchantment chip.
